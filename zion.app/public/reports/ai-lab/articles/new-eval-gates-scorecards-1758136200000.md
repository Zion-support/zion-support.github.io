# Eval Gates and Scorecards: Operationalizing Quality, Cost, and Risk

Eval gates convert aspirations into enforceable standards. This field guide shows how to:

- Define rubrics and thresholds per route and use case
- Attach gates to rollouts with canary/shadow strategies
- Build live scorecards that blend quality, latency, and spend
- Capture signed evidence for audits and fast incident analysis

Includes reference KPIs, sample thresholds, and a minimal scorecard schema.
