Safety drills validate that controls work under realistic conditions. This playbook shows how to run quarterly exercises that simulate risky tool‑use, policy exceptions, and degraded models, capturing signed evidence for each step.

Drill modules:

1. Permission boundary violations and remediation latency
2. Canary rollback triggers from quality regressions
3. Budget overrun detection and safe degradation

Measure drill success with time‑to‑detect, time‑to‑remediate, and evidence completeness. Use the findings to update policies, thresholds, and runbooks.
