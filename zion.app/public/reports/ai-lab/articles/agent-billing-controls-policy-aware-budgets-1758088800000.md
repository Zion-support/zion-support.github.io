# Agent Billing Controls — Policy‑Aware Budgets

Practical controls to cap spend while preserving quality, using policy‑aware routing, rate limits, and tiered capabilities.

## Controls

- Budget envelopes per team and environment
- Quotas and throttles on expensive tools and models
- Graceful fallbacks with outcome‑aware routing
- Alerting on cost posture changes and drift

Tie these controls to eval gates and SLOs for predictable costs.
