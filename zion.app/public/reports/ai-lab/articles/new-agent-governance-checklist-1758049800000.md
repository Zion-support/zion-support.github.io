# Agent Governance Checklist (2026 Preview)

Production agents require clear guardrails and verifiable controls. This checklist distills practical items teams can adopt today.

## Core Controls

- Policy-as-code with versioned approvals
- Evidence bundles for risky tool use
- Shadow testing before traffic exposure
- Live eval gates tied to SLOs

## Operational Readiness

- Incident runbooks with automated evidence capture
- RBAC for tools, prompts, and datasets
- Budgeted quality with cost/latency trade-offs

## Next Steps

Adopt one control per week, starting with eval gates and evidence bundles, then expand to policy automation.
