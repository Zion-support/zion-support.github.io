# Cost-Aware Model Routing: A 2026 Checklist

A practical routing checklist for production systems:

- Define outcomes and acceptable quality thresholds
- Capture token, latency, and success metrics per route
- Establish guardrails and fallback routes
- Use live evals to validate changes before rollout
- Optimize for cost-per-success, not token cost alone

Version routes with signed configs and attach evidence bundles for auditability.
