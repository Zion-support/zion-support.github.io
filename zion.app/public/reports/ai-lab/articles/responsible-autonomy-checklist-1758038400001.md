This checklist distills pragmatic controls for deploying autonomous agents safely in production. It focuses on measurable, auditable practices that reduce risk without stifling velocity.

Checklist:

- Policy-as-code with exception workflows and time-bound approvals
- Evals in the loop for critical actions and model changes
- Data minimization, redaction, and purpose limitation
- Tamper-evident evidence collection with export profiles (SOC2/ISO)
- Incident simulation and runbooks with MTTR targets
- Cost and latency budgets per route with alerts on drift

Adopting this checklist aligns engineering and compliance teams around shared, testable guarantees for safety and performance.
