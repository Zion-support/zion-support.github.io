Evidence‑led operations requires objective signals, enforceable policies, and signed artifacts. This guide outlines a pragmatic scorecard that connects SLOs (latency, quality, cost) with evaluation gates and incident evidence bundles.

Key elements:

1. SLOs with objective thresholds and error budgets
2. Evaluation gates mapped to the SLOs with pass/fail criteria
3. Policy‑as‑code checks for permissions, tool scopes, and exception handling
4. Evidence bundles that capture inputs, outputs, and lineage for audits

Adopt the scorecard in three steps: define SLOs, wire eval gates, and continuously export signed evidence bundles for reviews and postmortems.
