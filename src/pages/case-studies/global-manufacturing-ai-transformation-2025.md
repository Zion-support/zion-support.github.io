#### 2. Quality Control Automation
- **Computer Vision**: AI-powered visual inspection systems

- **Real-time Monitoring**: Continuous quality assessment during production
- **Automated Corrections**: Self-adjusting manufacturing parameters

#### 3. Supply Chain Optimization
- **Demand Forecasting**: AI models predicting component and material needs
- **Inventory Management**: Optimized stock levels reducing waste
- **Supplier Performance**: Real-time monitoring of supplier quality and delivery
- **Risk Assessment**: Predictive analysis of supply chain disruptions

#### 4. Production Planning and Scheduling
- **Dynamic Scheduling**: AI-optimized production schedules
- **Resource Allocation**: Intelligent assignment of equipment and personnel
- **Bottleneck Identification**: Proactive identification and resolution of production constraints
- **Capacity Optimization**: Maximizing throughput while maintaining quality
