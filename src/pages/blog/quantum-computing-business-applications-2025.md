## The Quantum Advantage: Understanding the Business Impact

### What Makes Quantum Computing Different

Quantum computers operate on fundamentally different principles than classical computers:

- **Quantum Superposition**: Processing multiple possibilities simultaneously
- **Quantum Entanglement**: Instantaneous correlation between quantum particles
- **Quantum Interference**: Amplifying correct answers while canceling incorrect ones
