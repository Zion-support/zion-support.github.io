## Understanding Quantum AI Superintelligence

### The Quantum Advantage in AI

Quantum computers leverage quantum mechanical phenomena—superposition, entanglement, and interference—to process information in ways impossible for classical systems:

**Superposition**: Quantum bits (qubits) exist in multiple states simultaneously, enabling parallel computation of exponentially many possibilities.

**Entanglement**: Qubits can be correlated in ways that classical bits cannot, enabling complex pattern recognition and optimization.

**Quantum Interference**: Quantum algorithms can amplify correct answers while canceling out incorrect ones, dramatically improving accuracy.

    # Convert graph to QUBO problem

        qubo_matrix[i][i] -= 1

        qubo_matrix[i][j] += 1
