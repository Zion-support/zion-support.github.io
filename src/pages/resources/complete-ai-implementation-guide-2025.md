### 1. Unrealistic Expectations
- **Pitfall**: Expecting immediate, dramatic results
- **Solution**: Set realistic timelines and celebrate incremental progress

### 2. Poor Data Quality
- **Pitfall**: Implementing AI with dirty or incomplete data
- **Solution**: Invest in data quality before AI implementation

### 3. Lack of Change Management
- **Pitfall**: Not preparing staff for AI adoption
- **Solution**: Comprehensive training and communication programs

### 4. Choosing the Wrong Solutions
- **Pitfall**: Implementing AI that doesn't match business needs
- **Solution**: Thorough needs assessment and vendor evaluation

### 5. Insufficient Security
- **Pitfall**: Overlooking AI system security requirements
- **Solution**: Implement robust security measures from day one
