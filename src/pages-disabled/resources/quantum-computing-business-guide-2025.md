## Understanding Quantum Computing

### What Makes Quantum Computing Different?

Traditional computers use bits (0 or 1), while quantum computers use quantum bits (qubits) that can exist in multiple states simultaneously. This fundamental difference enables:

- **Exponential Speedup**: Solving certain problems exponentially faster
- **Parallel Processing**: Processing multiple scenarios simultaneously
- **Optimization**: Finding optimal solutions to complex problems
- **Simulation**: Modeling quantum systems and complex interactions
