Classic release checklists do not translate to autonomous systems. Evidence‑first release engineering elevates measurable outcomes: quality, safety, reliability, and cost. This v2 playbook shows how to wire scorecards to CI preflight, canary rollout, real‑time monitoring, and rollback.

What’s new in v2:

- Minimal scorecard set aligned to service SLOs
- Policy events and eval traces bundled into portable evidence
- Rollback decision points with guardrails that maintain velocity

Case studies demonstrate reduced incidents, faster MTTR, and predictable spend.
