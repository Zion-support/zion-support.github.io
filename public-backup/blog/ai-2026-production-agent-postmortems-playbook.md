# Production Agent Postmortems — Playbook (2026)

Turn incidents into durable improvements through evidence-driven, blameless postmortems.

## Principles

- Blame-free analysis focused on systems, not people
- Evidence bundles: traces, evals, policy events, incidents
- Action items tied to guardrails and SLOs

## Template

1. Summary and customer impact
2. Timeline with key signals and decisions
3. Contributing factors (technical, process, organizational)
4. Remediations and prevention with owners and due dates
5. Follow-up validation via evals and game days
