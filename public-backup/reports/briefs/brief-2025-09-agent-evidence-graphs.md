# Agent Evidence Graphs — Executive Brief

Evidence graphs unify operational signals so you can explain outcomes and make safer decisions.

- Nodes: evaluations, spans, artifacts, policy events, incidents
- Edges: causedBy, derivedFrom, approves, rolledBackBy
- Outputs: scorecards, rollbacks, audit-ready timelines

Adopt a minimal schema first, expand with usage, and wire into change windows.
