## Challenges and Solutions

### Challenge 1: Physician Adoption
**Problem**: Initial resistance from some physicians
**Solution**: 
- Physician champion program
- Evidence-based demonstrations
- Gradual rollout with pilot groups
- Continuous feedback and improvement

### Challenge 2: Data Quality
**Problem**: Inconsistent data across systems
**Solution**:
- Data governance framework
- Quality monitoring systems
- Standardization initiatives
- Regular audits and improvements

### Challenge 3: Regulatory Compliance
**Problem**: Complex healthcare regulations
**Solution**:
- Legal and compliance team involvement
- Regular audits and assessments
- FDA guidance adherence
- Privacy and security controls

### Challenge 4: Integration Complexity
**Problem**: Complex EHR and system integration
**Solution**:
- API-first architecture
- Phased integration approach
- Dedicated integration team
- Vendor collaboration

## Lessons Learned

### What Worked Well
1. **Clinical Leadership**: Strong physician leadership was crucial
2. **Phased Approach**: Gradual implementation reduced risk
3. **Training Investment**: Comprehensive staff training paid off
4. **Patient Focus**: Keeping patients at center drove success
5. **Technology Integration**: Seamless workflow integration was key

### Areas for Improvement
1. **Change Management**: Earlier and more intensive change management
2. **Data Preparation**: More upfront data quality work
3. **Communication**: More frequent stakeholder updates
4. **Metrics**: Better baseline measurement
5. **Vendor Management**: Stronger vendor partnerships
