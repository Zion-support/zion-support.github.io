## Business Challenge

### The Crisis Point

By early 2024, the organization faced mounting challenges:

- **Legacy Systems**: 30+ year-old manufacturing systems causing bottlenecks
- **Quality Issues**: Defect rates approaching 8%, costing $2.3B annually
- **Downtime Crisis**: Equipment failures causing $4.5B in lost production
- **Supply Chain Chaos**: Inventory inefficiencies resulting in $3.2B waste
- **Energy Costs**: Suboptimal operations consuming $1.8B in excess energy
- **Competitive Pressure**: Losing market share to more agile competitors

### Specific Pain Points

1. **Production Inefficiency**
   - Manual quality inspections missing critical defects
   - Equipment maintenance performed on fixed schedules, not actual need
   - Production line stoppages averaging 15% of operating time
   - Inability to respond quickly to demand changes

2. **Supply Chain Fragmentation**
   - Disconnected systems across global facilities
   - Poor visibility into inventory levels and supply needs
   - Excess inventory in some locations, shortages in others
   - Long lead times for materials and components

3. **Quality Control Gaps**
   - 8% defect rate with high rework costs
   - Customer complaints increasing 35% year-over-year
   - Warranty claims costing $900M annually
   - Brand reputation at risk

4. **Resource Optimization**
   - Energy consumption 40% above industry benchmarks
   - Labor productivity declining due to manual processes
   - Equipment utilization at only 65% of capacity
   - Inability to predict and prevent equipment failures
