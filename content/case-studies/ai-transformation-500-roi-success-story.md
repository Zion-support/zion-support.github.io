**Company**: TechCorp Global

**Challenge**: Manual processes causing inefficiencies and high operational costs

2. **High Operational Costs**
   - $300M annually in manual processing costs

   - High customer service costs

3. **Customer Experience Issues**
   - Long response times (24-48 hours)
   - Inconsistent service quality
   - Limited self-service options
   - High customer churn rate (25%)

4. **Competitive Pressure**
   - Market share declining
   - Competitors gaining advantage through automation
   - Pressure to reduce prices
   - Need for faster innovation cycles
