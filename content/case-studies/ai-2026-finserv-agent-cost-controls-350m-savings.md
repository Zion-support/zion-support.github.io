# Global FinServ Saves $350M with Agent Cost Controls

A top‑10 financial services firm deployed agent cost scorecards, policy gates, and monthly executive reviews to reduce spend by 35–40% while maintaining quality via evals.

## Highlights

- $350M annualized savings
- Gate pass rate > 95%
- Quality preserved, latency stable

## Approach

1. Baseline cost per journey and route classes
2. Define budget bands with automatic reroute rules
3. Gate releases with cost + quality thresholds
4. Publish scorecards for Finance and Engineering

## Outcomes

- Spend variance contained; anomaly alerts reduced MTTR
- Finance and Engineering alignment through shared dashboards
- Repeatable model routing playbook across lines of business
