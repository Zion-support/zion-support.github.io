## Lessons Learned

### What Worked Well
1. **Executive Sponsorship**: Strong leadership support was critical
2. **Phased Approach**: Gradual implementation reduced risks
3. **Employee Engagement**: Involving employees in the process
4. **Data Quality**: Investing in data quality from the start
5. **Change Management**: Comprehensive training and support

### What Could Be Improved
1. **Timeline**: Could have been more aggressive with timeline
2. **Communication**: Earlier and more frequent communication
3. **Testing**: More extensive testing before full deployment
4. **Vendor Management**: Better vendor selection and management
5. **Documentation**: More comprehensive documentation

### Best Practices

3. **Iterate Quickly**: Rapid iteration and improvement
4. **Focus on Value**: Prioritize high-value use cases
