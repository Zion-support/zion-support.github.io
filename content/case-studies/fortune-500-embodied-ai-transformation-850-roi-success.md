### Technology Selection

**Embodied AI Platform**: Advanced Manufacturing Robotics Suite
- **Hardware**: 47 articulated robotic arms with 7-DOF manipulation
- **AI Software**: Multi-modal perception and adaptive learning algorithms
- **Integration**: Seamless connection with existing ERP and MES systems
- **Safety Systems**: Advanced collision avoidance and human-robot collaboration

**Key Capabilities**:
- **Precision Assembly**: ±0.05mm accuracy for critical components
- **Adaptive Learning**: Continuous improvement through operation data
- **Quality Inspection**: Real-time defect detection and correction
- **Flexible Manufacturing**: Rapid reconfiguration for different products

### Implementation Strategy

**Phase 1: Pilot Program (Months 1-3)**
- **Scope**: Single production line with 8 embodied AI systems
- **Objectives**: Validate technology, measure performance, build expertise
- **Investment**: $1.2M for pilot implementation
- **Team**: 15-person cross-functional implementation team

**Phase 2: Scale-Up (Months 4-8)**
- **Scope**: 12 additional production lines across 3 facilities
- **Investment**: $4.8M for expanded deployment
- **Training**: 240 operators trained on new systems
- **Integration**: Full connectivity with enterprise systems

**Phase 3: Full Deployment (Months 9-12)**
- **Scope**: All 47 manufacturing facilities globally
- **Investment**: $2.5M for remaining locations
- **Standardization**: Unified processes and procedures
- **Optimization**: Continuous improvement initiatives
