## Lessons Learned

### What Worked Well
1. **Pilot-first approach:** Validated technology before large-scale investment
2. **Cross-functional teams:** Brought together quantum physicists and software engineers
3. **Infrastructure investment:** Built robust foundation for quantum systems
4. **Change management:** Comprehensive training and communication

### Challenges Overcome
1. **Quantum decoherence:** Implemented advanced error correction
2. **Integration complexity:** Developed hybrid orchestration layer
3. **Skill gaps:** Intensive training programs for 2,400+ engineers
4. **Legacy systems:** Gradual migration strategy minimized disruption

### Recommendations for Others
1. **Start small:** Prove value with focused pilots
2. **Invest in talent:** Quantum skills are scarce but critical
3. **Partner strategically:** Leverage external expertise
4. **Plan for scale:** Build architecture with growth in mind
5. **Measure rigorously:** Track both technical and business metrics
