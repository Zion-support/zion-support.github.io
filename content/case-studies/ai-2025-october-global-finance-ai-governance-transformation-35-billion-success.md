## Lessons Learned

### What Worked Well

1. **Phased Approach**: Incremental implementation reduced risk and allowed for learning
2. **Technology Investment**: Platform automation was critical to scaling governance
3. **Culture Change**: Focus on ethics and responsibility drove lasting transformation
4. **Training**: Comprehensive education program ensured adoption and effectiveness

### Challenges Overcome

1. **Change Resistance**: Addressed through executive sponsorship and clear benefits communication
2. **Resource Constraints**: Justified through business case and phased implementation
3. **Technical Complexity**: Managed through expert partnership and platform selection
4. **Regulatory Uncertainty**: Addressed through proactive engagement and flexible framework
