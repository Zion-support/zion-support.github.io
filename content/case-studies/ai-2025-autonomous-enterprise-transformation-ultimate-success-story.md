## The Challenge

- **High Operational Costs**: Excessive overhead and inefficiencies
- **Quality Issues**: Inconsistent quality due to manual processes
- **Customer Dissatisfaction**: Delays and errors affecting customer experience

1. **Production Planning**: Manual scheduling leading to inefficiencies and delays
2. **Inventory Management**: Poor visibility and control over inventory levels
3. **Quality Control**: Inconsistent quality due to manual inspection processes

5. **Customer Service**: Slow response times and inconsistent service quality
6. **Financial Management**: Manual processes leading to errors and delays
