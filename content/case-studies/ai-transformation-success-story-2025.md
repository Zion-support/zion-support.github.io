## Lessons Learned

### Success Factors

1. **Executive Sponsorship**: Strong leadership support was crucial
2. **Change Management**: Comprehensive training and communication
3. **Phased Approach**: Gradual implementation reduced risks

5. **Plan for Scale**: Design systems for enterprise-wide deployment
