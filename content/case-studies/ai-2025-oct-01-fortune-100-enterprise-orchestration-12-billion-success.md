## Lessons Learned

### What Worked Well
1. **Platform-first approach** rather than point solutions
2. **Strong governance** from day one
3. **Iterative rollout** with quick wins
4. **Investment in training** and change management
5. **Executive visibility** through real-time dashboards

### Challenges Overcome
1. **Legacy system integration** - Solved with custom adapters
2. **Data quality issues** - Implemented automated cleaning
3. **Cultural resistance** - Addressed with training and quick wins
4. **Skill gaps** - Upskilled internal teams vs. only hiring
5. **Compliance complexity** - Built governance into platform

### Best Practices
1. Start with **business outcomes**, not technology
2. Measure **value created**, not just efficiency
3. Build **reusable workflows** and templates
4. Foster **cross-business unit collaboration**
5. Continuously **optimize and innovate**
