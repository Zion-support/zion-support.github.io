## Lessons Learned

### What Worked Exceptionally Well

1. **Edge-First Approach**: Deploying AI at the edge enabled <1ms latency
2. **Phased Rollout**: Gradual deployment reduced risk and enabled learning
3. **Comprehensive Training**: Investing heavily in training drove adoption
4. **Strong Governance**: Clear accountability ensured project success
5. **Partnership Model**: Close collaboration with Zion Tech Group accelerated delivery

### Challenges Overcome

1. **Legacy Integration**: Solved with API abstraction layers and adapters
2. **Network Latency**: Addressed through edge computing and network optimization
3. **Data Quality**: Resolved with automated quality frameworks
4. **Change Resistance**: Overcome through training and demonstrating value
5. **Scale Complexity**: Managed through phased approach and automation

### Best Practices for Others

1. **Start with Pilot**: Prove value before full deployment
2. **Invest in Infrastructure**: Foundation is critical for success
