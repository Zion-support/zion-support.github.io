### What Worked

3. **Comprehensive Training**: Ensured high adoption rates
4. **Phased Approach**: Reduced risk while maintaining momentum

1. **Legacy System Integration**: Solved with neural integration layer
2. **Data Quality Issues**: Addressed through automated cleansing
