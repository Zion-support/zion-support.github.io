## Lessons Learned

### What Worked Well

**1. Phased Approach**
- Pilot program built confidence and refined processes
- Gradual rollout reduced disruption
- Continuous feedback loop enabled improvements

**2. Advisor Empowerment**
- AI positioned as advisor enhancement, not replacement
- Advisors maintained client relationships and final decisions
- Technology handled routine tasks, freeing advisors for high-value work

**3. Client Transparency**
- Clear communication about AI role
- Emphasis on advisor + AI partnership
- Client education on benefits
- Interactive demonstrations

### Challenges & Solutions

**Challenge:** Advisor resistance to change
- **Solution:** Pilot program with champions, visible success stories, comprehensive training

**Challenge:** Data quality issues (legacy systems)
- **Solution:** Automated data validation, client review workflows, manual correction processes

**Challenge:** Integration complexity (12 existing systems)
- **Solution:** API-first architecture, phased integration, vendor partnerships

**Challenge:** Regulatory uncertainty (AI disclosure requirements)
- **Solution:** Conservative compliance approach, legal counsel involvement, continuous monitoring
