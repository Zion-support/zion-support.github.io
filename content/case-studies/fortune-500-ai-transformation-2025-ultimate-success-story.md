## Lessons Learned

### What Worked
1. **Executive Sponsorship**: Critical for success and resource allocation
2. **Phased Implementation**: Reduced risk and enabled learning
3. **Data Quality Investment**: Foundation for all AI success
4. **Change Management**: Comprehensive approach to adoption

### What Didn't Work
1. **Big Bang Approach**: Initial attempt at enterprise-wide deployment failed
2. **Technology-First**: Early focus on technology instead of business outcomes
3. **Insufficient Training**: Initial training programs were inadequate
4. **Data Silos**: Early failure to integrate data across systems
5. **Resistance Management**: Underestimated change management requirements
