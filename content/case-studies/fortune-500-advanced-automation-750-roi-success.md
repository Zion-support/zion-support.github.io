1. **Visual Inspection Bottlenecks**

2. **Data Management Issues**
   - Quality data scattered across multiple systems
   - No real-time visibility into quality metrics

   - Difficulty identifying root causes of defects

3. **Compliance Challenges**
   - Manual compliance documentation
   - Risk of regulatory violations
   - Inconsistent audit preparation
