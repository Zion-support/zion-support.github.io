## Lessons Learned

### What Worked Well

1. **Start with Quick Wins**: Early bias fixes built credibility and momentum
2. **Automate Everything**: 94% automation made scale feasible
3. **Make it Easy**: Integrated ethics into existing MLOps workflows
4. **Measure Relentlessly**: What gets measured gets managed
5. **Celebrate Success**: Public recognition of ethical AI development

### Challenges Overcome

1. **Scale**: Purpose-built platform handled 47K decisions/second
2. **Legacy Systems**: Adapters and APIs integrated diverse systems
3. **Resistance**: Demonstrated ROI and competitive advantage
4. **Complexity**: Risk-based approach prioritized high-impact work

### Recommendations for Others

1. **Start Now**: Regulatory and customer pressure will only increase
2. **Think Big, Start Small**: Pilot on high-risk systems first
3. **Invest in Technology**: Manual approaches don't scale
4. **Engage Stakeholders**: Ethics can't be done in isolation
5. **Make it Business-Focused**: Frame as value creation, not compliance
