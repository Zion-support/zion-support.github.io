**1. Starting with High-Impact Areas**

- Immediate visible impact
- Built momentum quickly

**2. Automation First Approach**
- Eliminated manual bottlenecks

**3. Developer Enablement**
- Security education integrated into development
- Tools that enhance productivity
- Positive reinforcement

**4. Metrics-Driven Approach**
- Clear KPIs established upfront
- Regular performance tracking
- Data-driven decision making
