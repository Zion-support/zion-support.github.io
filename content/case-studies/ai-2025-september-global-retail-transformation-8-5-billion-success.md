The client faced significant competitive pressure and needed to modernize:

- **Inefficient Supply Chain:** 28% stockout rate costing $2.3B annually

- **Fragmented Data:** 47 disconnected systems preventing unified customer view
- **Limited Automation:** 85% of operational processes still manual

### Business Objectives:
- Reduce stockouts and optimize inventory
- Increase customer conversion and lifetime value
- Implement dynamic pricing optimization
- Unify data infrastructure for AI-driven insights
- Automate operations at scale

## Lessons Learned

### What Worked Well:
- Starting with high-impact use cases (supply chain, pricing)
- Building strong data foundation before scaling AI
- Empowering business teams with self-service AI tools
- Maintaining focus on business outcomes over technology

### Challenges Overcome:
- Legacy system integration complexity
- Data quality issues requiring significant cleanup
- Organizational resistance to automation
- Skill gaps requiring extensive training
