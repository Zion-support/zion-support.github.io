## Lessons Learned

### What Worked Well
1. **Pilot-First Approach:** Proving ROI before enterprise rollout secured buy-in
2. **Business-Led Implementation:** Use cases driven by business needs, not IT
3. **Continuous Optimization:** Regular model retraining and feature enhancements
4. **User-Centric Design:** Natural language interface critical for adoption

### Challenges Overcome
1. **Data Quality Issues:** Implemented automated data cleansing and validation
2. **Change Resistance:** Addressed through training and visible executive support
3. **Integration Complexity:** Resolved with phased approach and dedicated team
4. **Scale Performance:** Optimized architecture for 50,000+ concurrent users
