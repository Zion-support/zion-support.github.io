   - Difficulty tracking decision rationale

3. **Compliance and Risk Management**
   - Manual compliance checking taking 2-3 days

   - Inconsistent risk assessment across regions
   - High cost of compliance management

4. **Customer Experience Issues**
   - Long wait times for loan decisions

   - High customer churn rate
