**Global Healthcare Network**

**Quality & Consistency:**
- Variability in diagnostic standards across facilities

- Inconsistent application of clinical guidelines

### Business Impact
- **$420M annual costs** from diagnostic errors and delays

## Lessons Learned

### What Worked Well
1. **Pilot-first approach**: Validated value before large-scale investment
2. **Physician collaboration**: Built trust through transparency and co-development
3. **Incremental deployment**: Reduced risk and allowed for learning
4. **Continuous improvement**: ML pipeline for ongoing model enhancement
5. **User experience focus**: Seamless workflow integration drove adoption

### Challenges Overcome
1. **Data quality variability**: Implemented comprehensive data validation
2. **Legacy system integration**: Built flexible integration layer
3. **Physician skepticism**: Demonstrated value through pilot results
4. **Regulatory complexity**: Proactive engagement with regulators
5. **Change resistance**: Extensive training and change management
