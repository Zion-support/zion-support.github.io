# Neural Interface Revolution: How Brain-Computer Interfaces Are Transforming Business Operations

## The Dawn of Neural Interfaces

Neural interfaces, also known as brain-computer interfaces (BCIs), represent the next frontier in human-computer interaction. These revolutionary technologies are already transforming how businesses operate, enhancing productivity, and creating new possibilities for human-machine collaboration.

## What Are Neural Interfaces?

Neural interfaces are direct communication pathways between the brain and external devices. They can:

- **Read brain signals** to control computers and devices
- **Stimulate neural activity** for therapeutic or enhancement purposes
- **Enable thought-based communication** for individuals with disabilities
- **Augment human capabilities** in professional environments
