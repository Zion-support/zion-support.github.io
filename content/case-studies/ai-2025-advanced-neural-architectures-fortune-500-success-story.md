## Lessons Learned

### What Worked Well
1. **Phased Approach**: Gradual rollout reduced risk and improved adoption
2. **Executive Support**: Strong leadership commitment was crucial

### Challenges Overcome

2. **Skill Gaps**: Extensive training programs built internal capabilities

4. **Technical Complexity**: Expert partnerships provided necessary expertise

2. **Measure Everything**: Comprehensive metrics enabled continuous improvement
3. **Focus on ROI**: Prioritized high-impact use cases for maximum return
4. **Plan for Scale**: Designed systems to handle future growth and expansion
