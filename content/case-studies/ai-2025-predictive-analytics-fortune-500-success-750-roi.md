## Lessons Learned

### Success Factors
1. **Executive Sponsorship**: Strong leadership support was crucial
2. **Data Quality**: Investing in data quality upfront paid dividends
3. **Change Management**: Comprehensive training and support programs
4. **Phased Approach**: Gradual rollout reduced risk and improved adoption
5. **Continuous Improvement**: Regular model updates and optimization

### Challenges Overcome
1. **Data Silos**: Extensive integration work required
2. **Legacy Systems**: Gradual migration and modernization
3. **User Adoption**: Training and support programs essential
4. **Technical Complexity**: Expert team and external support needed
5. **Change Management**: Clear communication and stakeholder engagement

### Best Practices
1. **Start Small**: Pilot projects before full-scale deployment
2. **Measure Everything**: Comprehensive metrics and monitoring
3. **Iterate Quickly**: Rapid development and deployment cycles
4. **Focus on Value**: Business impact over technical complexity
5. **Invest in People**: Training and development programs
