## Lessons Learned

### What Worked Well

1. **Pilot-First Approach**: Proving value before large-scale investment
2. **Cross-Functional Teams**: Business and IT working together
3. **Continuous Communication**: Regular updates to all stakeholders
4. **Metric-Driven**: Clear success criteria and tracking
5. **Expert Partnership**: Leveraging specialized expertise

### What We'd Do Differently

1. **Start Larger**: Pilot could have been bigger given success
2. **More Training**: Even more upfront skill development
3. **Faster Rollout**: Accelerate deployment to capture value sooner
4. **Broader Scope**: Include more processes from beginning
5. **Earlier Innovation**: Launch innovation programs sooner
