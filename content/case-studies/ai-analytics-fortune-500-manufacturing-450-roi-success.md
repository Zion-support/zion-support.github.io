## Lessons Learned

### What Worked Well
1. **Phased Approach:** Gradual implementation reduced risk
2. **Data Quality Focus:** High-quality data enabled accurate models
3. **User Involvement:** Early user involvement improved adoption
4. **Executive Support:** Strong leadership support was crucial

### Challenges Overcome
1. **Data Integration:** Complex integration across 150+ facilities
2. **Change Management:** Organizational resistance to new processes
3. **Model Accuracy:** Initial models required significant tuning
4. **System Performance:** Scalability challenges with large datasets

### Best Practices Identified
1. **Start with High-Impact Use Cases:** Focus on areas with clear ROI
2. **Invest in Data Quality:** Foundation for successful AI implementation
3. **Plan for Change Management:** Organizational change is critical
4. **Monitor and Iterate:** Continuous improvement is essential
