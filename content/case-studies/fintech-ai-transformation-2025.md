## Lessons Learned

### What Worked Well
1. Phased implementation approach with clear milestones
2. Strong executive sponsorship and cross-functional alignment
3. Focus on customer experience alongside operational efficiency
4. Investment in change management and employee training
5. Continuous learning and optimization of AI models

### Key Challenges Overcome
1. Legacy system integration and data migration complexity
2. Regulatory approval processes across multiple jurisdictions
3. Employee concerns about AI replacing human roles
4. Customer trust in AI-powered financial advice
5. Technical complexity of achieving 99.99% uptime

### Recommendations for Others
1. Start with clear business objectives tied to measurable outcomes
2. Invest heavily in data quality and governance from day one
3. Build internal AI capabilities while leveraging external expertise
4. Prioritize security, compliance, and ethical AI practices
5. Maintain focus on customer value throughout transformation
