## Lessons Learned

### What Worked
1. **Start with High-Impact Processes**: Focus on processes with highest ROI potential
2. **Invest in Training**: Comprehensive training is essential for success
3. **Change Management**: Proper change management prevents resistance
4. **Continuous Monitoring**: Real-time monitoring enables quick optimization

### What to Avoid
1. **Rushing Implementation**: Take time to properly plan and execute
2. **Ignoring Change Management**: Employee buy-in is crucial
3. **Poor Integration**: Ensure seamless integration with existing systems
4. **Lack of Monitoring**: Continuous monitoring is essential for success
