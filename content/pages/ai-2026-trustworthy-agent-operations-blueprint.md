This blueprint standardizes autonomous agent operations across safety, reliability, security, and cost.

Core components:

- SLOs for safety, quality, reliability, and cost
- Evaluation lifecycle: pre-ship, canary, runtime, regression nets
- Policy-as-code for tools, permissions, and approvals
- Observability: traces, spans, eval IDs, policy decisions
- Incident response: runbooks, mitigation patterns, auto-rollback

Adoption path (90 days): define SLOs, wire evals, enforce policy gates, and publish evidence.

Expected outcomes: 40% incident reduction, <10m MTTR, 20–40% cost optimization.
