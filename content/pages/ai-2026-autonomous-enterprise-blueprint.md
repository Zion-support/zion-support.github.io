# AI 2026: Autonomous Enterprise Blueprint

Design, govern, and scale autonomous systems with policy-as-code guardrails, closed-loop observability, and outcome-driven KPIs.

## Key Outcomes

- Outcome-driven autonomy with policy-as-code guardrails
- Closed-loop observability and human-on-the-loop controls
- Reference operating model and rollout plan

## First 90 Days

Start with a value-focused pilot, instrument KPIs, implement agentic workflows with approval gates, and iterate to measurable business impact.
