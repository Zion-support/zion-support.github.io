## Quantum ML Fundamentals

### What is Quantum Machine Learning?

Quantum machine learning combines quantum computing principles with machine learning algorithms to solve problems that are intractable for classical computers. Key concepts include:

#### Quantum Computing Principles
**Superposition:**
- Quantum bits (qubits) exist in multiple states simultaneously
- Enables parallel processing of exponential data
- Provides computational advantage for specific problems

**Entanglement:**
- Qubits can be correlated across vast distances
- Enables quantum communication and teleportation
- Provides exponential information processing capacity

**Interference:**
- Quantum states can interfere constructively or destructively
- Enables quantum algorithms to amplify correct solutions
- Provides exponential speedup for optimization problems

#### Quantum ML Algorithms
**Quantum Neural Networks:**
- Quantum versions of classical neural networks
- Exponential speedup for specific learning tasks
- Enhanced pattern recognition capabilities

**Quantum Support Vector Machines:**
