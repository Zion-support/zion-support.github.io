## Risk Management

### 1. Technical Risks
**Data Quality Issues**
- Risk: Poor data quality affects AI performance
- Mitigation: Data governance framework, quality monitoring
- Monitoring: Regular data audits, quality metrics

**Integration Challenges**
- Risk: Legacy system integration failures
- Mitigation: API-first architecture, gradual migration
- Monitoring: Integration testing, performance metrics

**Security Vulnerabilities**
- Risk: AI systems vulnerable to attacks
- Mitigation: Security by design, regular audits
- Monitoring: Security monitoring, threat detection

### 2. Business Risks
**Change Resistance**
- Risk: Employee resistance to AI adoption
- Mitigation: Change management, training, communication
- Monitoring: Employee surveys, adoption metrics

**ROI Underperformance**
- Risk: AI investments don't deliver expected returns
- Mitigation: Careful pilot selection, realistic expectations
- Monitoring: Regular ROI reviews, course correction

**Competitive Disadvantage**
- Risk: Falling behind competitors in AI adoption
- Mitigation: Accelerated implementation, strategic partnerships
- Monitoring: Market analysis, competitive intelligence

### 3. Operational Risks
**System Downtime**
- Risk: AI system failures impact operations
- Mitigation: Redundancy, monitoring, quick recovery
- Monitoring: Uptime metrics, incident response

**Skill Shortages**
- Risk: Lack of AI talent to support implementation
- Mitigation: Training programs, external partnerships
- Monitoring: Skill assessments, hiring metrics
