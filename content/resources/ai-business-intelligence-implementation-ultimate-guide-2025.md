## Common Pitfalls

### 1. Insufficient Planning
**Problem**: Rushing into implementation without proper planning
**Solution**: Invest time in detailed planning and preparation
**Prevention**: Create comprehensive project plan with clear milestones

### 2. Poor Data Quality
**Problem**: Implementing AI on low-quality data
**Solution**: Invest heavily in data quality improvement
**Prevention**: Conduct thorough data audit before starting

### 3. User Resistance
**Problem**: Users resist new technology and processes
**Solution**: Invest in change management and training
**Prevention**: Involve users early and often in design process

### 4. Technology Over-Engineering
**Problem**: Building overly complex solutions
**Solution**: Start simple and add complexity gradually
**Prevention**: Focus on business value over technical features

### 5. Inadequate Testing
**Problem**: Insufficient testing leads to production issues
**Solution**: Implement comprehensive testing strategy
**Prevention**: Plan for extensive testing at each phase

### 6. Lack of Governance
**Problem**: No clear ownership or decision-making process
**Solution**: Establish clear governance structure
**Prevention**: Define roles and responsibilities upfront
