## Common Pitfalls and Solutions

### Pitfall 1: Lack of Executive Support
**Solution**: Secure strong executive sponsorship
- Present compelling business case
- Demonstrate clear ROI
- Engage executives in planning
- Maintain regular communication

### Pitfall 2: Poor Data Quality
**Solution**: Invest in data quality and governance
- Implement data governance framework
- Clean and validate data
- Establish data quality standards
- Regular data audits

### Pitfall 3: Insufficient Change Management
**Solution**: Comprehensive change management program
- Communicate benefits clearly
- Provide extensive training
- Address resistance proactively
- Celebrate early wins

### Pitfall 4: Technology-First Approach
**Solution**: Business-first approach
- Start with business needs
- Focus on user experience
- Ensure business alignment
- Measure business outcomes

### Pitfall 5: Unrealistic Expectations
**Solution**: Set realistic expectations
- Start with pilot projects
- Focus on incremental progress
- Manage stakeholder expectations
- Celebrate small wins
