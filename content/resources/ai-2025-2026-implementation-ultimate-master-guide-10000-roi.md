## ROI Framework Overview

### The 10,000% ROI Formula

```
ROI = (Total Benefits - Total Investment) / Total Investment × 100

Where:
Total Benefits = Revenue Increase + Cost Savings + Risk Mitigation + Efficiency Gains
Total Investment = Technology + Implementation + Training + Ongoing Costs
```
