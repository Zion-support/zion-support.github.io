## Understanding Autonomous Enterprises

### Definition and Characteristics

An autonomous enterprise is an organization that leverages advanced AI systems to operate with minimal human intervention across core business functions. Key characteristics include:

- **Self-Optimization**: Systems automatically improve performance
- **Self-Healing**: Automatic error detection and recovery
- **Self-Learning**: Continuous improvement from data patterns
- **Self-Scaling**: Dynamic resource allocation based on demand
- **Self-Securing**: Proactive threat detection and response
