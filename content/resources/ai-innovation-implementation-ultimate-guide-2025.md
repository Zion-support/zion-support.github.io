```
ROI = (Total Returns - Total Investment) / Total Investment × 100

Where:
Total Returns = Annual Cost Savings + Revenue Growth + Risk Reduction Value

Example:
Total Investment: $50M
Total Annual Returns: $2.5B
ROI = ($2.5B - $50M) / $50M × 100 = 4,900%
```
