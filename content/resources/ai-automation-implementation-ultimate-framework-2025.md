## ROI Calculation Framework

### Investment Components

**Technology Costs**:
- Software licenses and subscriptions
- Hardware and infrastructure
- Integration and customization
- Training and support

**Implementation Costs**:
- Consulting and professional services
- Internal resource allocation
- Change management and training
- Testing and validation

**Ongoing Costs**:
- Maintenance and support
- Updates and upgrades
- Monitoring and optimization
- Training and development

### Return Components

**Cost Savings**:
- Reduced manual labor costs
- Lower error and rework costs
- Decreased operational overhead
- Improved efficiency gains

**Revenue Impact**:
- Increased customer satisfaction
- Faster time to market
- New business opportunities
- Competitive advantage

**Risk Reduction**:
- Decreased compliance risks
- Lower operational risks
- Improved data security
- Enhanced business continuity

### ROI Calculation Formula

**ROI = (Total Benefits - Total Costs) / Total Costs × 100**

**Where**:
- **Total Benefits** = Cost Savings + Revenue Impact + Risk Reduction
- **Total Costs** = Technology + Implementation + Ongoing Costs
