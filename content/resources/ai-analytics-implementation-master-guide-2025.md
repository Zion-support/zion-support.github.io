#### 1. Technology-First Approach
- **Problem:** Focusing on technology before business needs
- **Solution:** Start with business objectives and use cases
- **Prevention:** Business-led approach with technology support

#### 2. Insufficient Data Quality
- **Problem:** Poor data quality leading to inaccurate models
- **Solution:** Invest in data quality and governance
- **Prevention:** Data quality assessment before implementation

#### 3. Lack of User Involvement
- **Problem:** Low user adoption due to poor user experience
- **Solution:** Involve users in design and testing
- **Prevention:** User-centered design approach

#### 4. Inadequate Change Management
- **Problem:** Resistance to change and low adoption
- **Solution:** Comprehensive change management program
- **Prevention:** Change management from project start

#### 5. Insufficient Monitoring
- **Problem:** Model performance degradation over time
- **Solution:** Implement comprehensive monitoring and alerting
- **Prevention:** Monitoring and maintenance planning
