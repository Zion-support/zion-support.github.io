## Common Pitfalls & How to Avoid Them

### 1. Technology-First Approach
**Pitfall**: Focusing on technology rather than business outcomes
**Avoidance**: Start with business objectives and work backward
**Solution**: Business-driven technology decisions

### 2. Insufficient Data Quality
**Pitfall**: Implementing AI on poor-quality data
**Avoidance**: Invest in data quality improvement first
**Solution**: Comprehensive data governance and quality monitoring

### 3. Lack of Change Management
**Pitfall**: Resistance to change and poor adoption
**Avoidance**: Invest in comprehensive change management
**Solution**: Training, communication, and support programs

### 4. Inadequate Security
**Pitfall**: Security vulnerabilities and compliance issues
**Avoidance**: Implement security-by-design principles
**Solution**: Comprehensive security and compliance framework

### 5. Poor Project Management
**Pitfall**: Timeline delays and budget overruns
**Avoidance**: Use proven project management methodologies
**Solution**: Agile development and continuous monitoring
