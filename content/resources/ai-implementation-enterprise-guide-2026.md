## Risk Management

### Technical Risks
1. **Data Quality Issues**
   - Risk: Poor data quality affecting AI model performance
   - Mitigation: Robust data governance and quality monitoring
   - Monitoring: Data quality metrics and alerts

2. **Model Performance Degradation**
   - Risk: AI models becoming less accurate over time
   - Mitigation: Continuous monitoring and retraining
   - Monitoring: Model performance metrics and drift detection

3. **Integration Challenges**
   - Risk: Difficulties integrating AI with existing systems
   - Mitigation: API-first architecture and phased integration
   - Monitoring: Integration success rates and performance

### Business Risks
1. **Resistance to Change**
   - Risk: Employee resistance to AI adoption
   - Mitigation: Comprehensive change management program
   - Monitoring: Employee engagement and adoption metrics

2. **Budget Overruns**
   - Risk: AI implementation costs exceeding budget
   - Mitigation: Phased implementation and regular budget reviews
   - Monitoring: Cost tracking and variance analysis

3. **Competitive Disadvantage**
   - Risk: Falling behind competitors in AI adoption
   - Mitigation: Accelerated implementation and continuous innovation
   - Monitoring: Market position and competitive analysis

### Regulatory Risks
1. **Compliance Violations**
   - Risk: Non-compliance with data protection regulations
   - Mitigation: Privacy-by-design and compliance monitoring
   - Monitoring: Compliance audits and risk assessments

2. **Ethical Concerns**
   - Risk: AI bias and fairness issues
   - Mitigation: Bias testing and ethical AI frameworks
   - Monitoring: Fairness metrics and ethical reviews
