## ROI Calculation and Measurement

### Financial Metrics

#### Direct Cost Savings
**Labor Cost Reduction**:
- Formula: (Current Labor Cost × Automation Percentage × Efficiency Gain)
- Example: $10M × 0.7 × 0.6 = $4.2M annual savings

**Operational Efficiency**:
- Formula: (Current Process Cost × Speed Improvement × Quality Improvement)
- Example: $5M × 0.4 × 0.3 = $600K annual savings

**Error Reduction**:
- Formula: (Current Error Cost × Error Reduction Percentage)
- Example: $2M × 0.85 = $1.7M annual savings

#### Revenue Generation
**Faster Time-to-Market**:
- Formula: (Additional Revenue × Time Savings × Market Opportunity)
- Example: $50M × 0.5 × 0.2 = $5M additional revenue

**Customer Satisfaction**:
- Formula: (Customer Retention Improvement × Average Customer Value)
- Example: 0.15 × $100M = $15M additional revenue

### ROI Calculation Framework

#### Investment Components
- **Technology Costs**: Software, hardware, cloud services
- **Implementation Costs**: Consulting, development, integration
- **Training Costs**: Employee training and certification
- **Change Management**: Communication, support, transition

#### Benefit Components
- **Direct Savings**: Labor, operational, maintenance costs
- **Revenue Generation**: New revenue, market share growth
- **Risk Mitigation**: Compliance, security, operational risks
- **Strategic Value**: Competitive advantage, innovation capability

#### ROI Formula
```
ROI = (Total Benefits - Total Investment) / Total Investment × 100%
```

**Example Calculation**:
- Total Investment: $5M
- Annual Benefits: $40M
- ROI: ($40M - $5M) / $5M × 100% = 700%
