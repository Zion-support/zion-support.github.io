#### Model Types and Applications

**Predictive Maintenance:**
- **Time Series Models:** LSTM, GRU, Transformer
- **Anomaly Detection:** Isolation Forest, One-Class SVM
- **Survival Analysis:** Cox Proportional Hazards
- **Ensemble Methods:** Random Forest, XGBoost

**Demand Forecasting:**
- **ARIMA Models:** Seasonal ARIMA, SARIMA
- **Deep Learning:** LSTM, GRU, Transformer
- **Ensemble Methods:** Stacking, Blending
- **Probabilistic Models:** Prophet, DeepAR

**Customer Behavior:**
- **Classification:** Logistic Regression, Random Forest
- **Clustering:** K-means, DBSCAN, Gaussian Mixture
- **Recommendation:** Collaborative Filtering, Matrix Factorization
- **Deep Learning:** Neural Networks, Autoencoders

#### Model Training Best Practices

**Data Splitting:**
- **Training Set:** 70% of data
- **Validation Set:** 15% of data
- **Test Set:** 15% of data
- **Time-based Splitting:** For time series data

**Cross-Validation:**
- **Time Series CV:** Walk-forward validation
- **Stratified CV:** For imbalanced datasets
- **Group CV:** For grouped data
- **Nested CV:** For hyperparameter tuning

**Hyperparameter Optimization:**
- **Grid Search:** Exhaustive search
- **Random Search:** Random sampling
- **Bayesian Optimization:** Gaussian Process
- **Evolutionary Algorithms:** Genetic algorithms
