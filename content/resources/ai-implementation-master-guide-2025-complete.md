#### 4. Model Deployment
- **Containerization**: Package model for deployment

## Common Pitfalls and Solutions

### 1. Data Quality Issues
**Problem**: Poor data quality leading to inaccurate AI models
**Solution**: Implement comprehensive data governance and quality controls
**Prevention**: Data audit and cleaning before AI implementation

### 2. Unrealistic Expectations
**Problem**: Expecting immediate results and 100% accuracy
**Solution**: Set realistic timelines and performance expectations
**Prevention**: Clear communication about AI capabilities and limitations

### 3. Lack of Change Management
**Problem**: Employee resistance and poor adoption
**Solution**: Invest in comprehensive training and change management
**Prevention**: Involve employees in the process from the beginning

### 4. Technology Over-Engineering
**Problem**: Choosing overly complex solutions for simple problems
**Solution**: Start with simple solutions and scale as needed
**Prevention**: Focus on business value over technical complexity

### 5. Insufficient Testing
**Problem**: Deploying AI systems without adequate testing
**Solution**: Implement comprehensive testing strategy
**Prevention**: Allocate sufficient time for testing and validation
