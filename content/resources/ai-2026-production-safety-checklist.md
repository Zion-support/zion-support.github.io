Use this checklist to standardize how teams design, validate, deploy, and operate AI systems at scale.

## Design
- Define risk tiers and data classifications
- Threat model tool access and side effects
- Establish SLOs and error budgets

## Validation
- Scenario suites with pass thresholds and coverage targets
- Guardrail tests (toxicity, privacy, safety)
- Shadow traffic and red‑team probes

## Deployment
- Evaluation gates in CI/CD with signed attestations
- Progressive delivery with automated rollback
- Rollout dashboard with live KPIs

## Operations
- Traces + eval spans + metrics + logs
- Incident runbook with triggers and escalation
- Weekly evidence reviews and drift watch

## Audit
- Immutable evidence bundles per change
- Approval trail: who, why, what changed
- Retention policy and access controls
