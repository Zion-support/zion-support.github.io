## Quantum AI Fundamentals

### What is Quantum AI?

Quantum AI combines quantum computing principles with artificial intelligence to solve problems that are intractable for classical computers. It leverages quantum phenomena like superposition and entanglement to process information in fundamentally new ways.

### Key Quantum Concepts

#### 1. Quantum Superposition
- **Definition**: Quantum bits (qubits) can exist in multiple states simultaneously
- **Business Impact**: Parallel processing of millions of scenarios
- **Use Cases**: Portfolio optimization, supply chain planning, risk assessment

#### 2. Quantum Entanglement
- **Definition**: Qubits can be correlated in ways that classical bits cannot
- **Business Impact**: Enhanced data correlation and pattern recognition
- **Use Cases**: Fraud detection, market analysis, customer behavior prediction

#### 3. Quantum Interference
- **Definition**: Quantum states can interfere constructively or destructively
- **Business Impact**: Amplification of correct solutions, suppression of errors
- **Use Cases**: Optimization algorithms, machine learning acceleration
