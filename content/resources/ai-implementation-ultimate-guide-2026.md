## Common Pitfalls & Solutions

### 7.1 Technical Pitfalls

**Data Quality Issues**:
- **Problem**: Poor data quality leads to poor AI performance
- **Solution**: Implement comprehensive data governance and quality controls
- **Prevention**: Start with data assessment and cleansing

**Integration Challenges**:
- **Problem**: Difficulty integrating AI with existing systems
- **Solution**: Use APIs and microservices architecture
- **Prevention**: Plan integration requirements early

**Scalability Problems**:
- **Problem**: AI systems can't handle increased load
- **Solution**: Design for scalability from the start
- **Prevention**: Use cloud-native architectures

### 7.2 Organizational Pitfalls

**Change Management Issues**:
- **Problem**: Resistance to AI adoption
- **Solution**: Comprehensive training and communication
- **Prevention**: Involve stakeholders early and often

**Skill Gaps**:
- **Problem**: Lack of AI expertise in the organization
- **Solution**: Partner with AI specialists and provide training
- **Prevention**: Assess skills early and plan accordingly

**Unrealistic Expectations**:
- **Problem**: Expecting immediate results
- **Solution**: Set realistic timelines and milestones
- **Prevention**: Educate stakeholders about AI implementation realities

### 7.3 Business Pitfalls

**Poor Use Case Selection**:
- **Problem**: Choosing low-impact or infeasible use cases
- **Solution**: Conduct thorough business impact analysis
- **Prevention**: Start with pilot programs

**Insufficient ROI Measurement**:
- **Problem**: Not measuring or tracking ROI effectively
- **Solution**: Implement comprehensive measurement framework
- **Prevention**: Define metrics before implementation

**Lack of Executive Support**:
- **Problem**: Insufficient leadership commitment
- **Solution**: Demonstrate value through pilots and quick wins
- **Prevention**: Secure executive buy-in early
