## Common Pitfalls

### Technical Pitfalls
**Inadequate Data Preparation:**
- **Issue**: Poor data quality leads to suboptimal AI performance
- **Solution**: Invest in comprehensive data cleaning and validation
- **Prevention**: Establish data quality monitoring from day one

**Over-Complex Architecture:**
- **Issue**: Overly complex systems are difficult to maintain and scale
- **Solution**: Start with simple, proven architectures and evolve
- **Prevention**: Focus on business value over technical complexity

**Insufficient Testing:**
- **Issue**: Inadequate testing leads to production failures
- **Solution**: Implement comprehensive testing at all levels
- **Prevention**: Establish testing as a core development practice

### Business Pitfalls
**Lack of Executive Support:**
- **Issue**: Insufficient leadership support leads to project failure
- **Solution**: Secure strong C-level sponsorship and budget approval
- **Prevention**: Demonstrate clear business value and ROI

**Poor Change Management:**
- **Issue**: Resistance to change leads to low adoption rates
- **Solution**: Implement comprehensive change management programs
- **Prevention**: Involve users early and often in the process

**Unrealistic Expectations:**
- **Issue**: Overpromising leads to disappointment and project failure
- **Solution**: Set realistic expectations and communicate clearly
- **Prevention**: Base expectations on proven use cases and metrics
