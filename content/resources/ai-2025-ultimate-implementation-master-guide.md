### ROI Calculation Formula

```
ROI = (Total Benefits - Total Investment) / Total Investment × 100

Where:
Total Benefits = Cost Savings + Revenue Growth + Efficiency Gains
Total Investment = Technology + Infrastructure + Services + Training
```
