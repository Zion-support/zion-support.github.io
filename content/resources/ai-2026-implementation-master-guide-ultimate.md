## Risk Management and Mitigation

### Technical Risks
1. **Data Quality Issues**
   - Risk: Poor data quality affecting AI performance
   - Mitigation: Implement data quality monitoring and improvement processes

2. **Integration Challenges**
   - Risk: Difficulties integrating AI with existing systems
   - Mitigation: Use standard APIs and integration platforms

3. **Performance Issues**
   - Risk: AI systems not meeting performance requirements
   - Mitigation: Implement comprehensive testing and monitoring

4. **Security Vulnerabilities**
   - Risk: AI systems vulnerable to security attacks
   - Mitigation: Implement robust security measures and monitoring

### Business Risks
1. **User Adoption**
   - Risk: Low user adoption of AI systems
   - Mitigation: Comprehensive training and change management

2. **Regulatory Compliance**
   - Risk: Non-compliance with AI regulations
   - Mitigation: Implement compliance monitoring and reporting

3. **Ethical Concerns**
   - Risk: AI systems causing ethical issues
   - Mitigation: Implement AI ethics framework and monitoring

4. **Vendor Dependencies**
   - Risk: Over-dependence on AI vendors
   - Mitigation: Diversify vendors and build internal capabilities
