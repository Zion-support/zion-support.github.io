### 2. ROI Calculation Formula

```
ROI = (Total Benefits - Total Investment) / Total Investment × 100

Where:
Total Benefits = Direct Savings + Revenue Impact + Indirect Benefits
Total Investment = Technology + Implementation + Training + Maintenance

Example Calculation:
Total Benefits = $2.8B (Direct) + $850M (Revenue) + $200M (Indirect) = $3.85B
Total Investment = $650M
ROI = ($3.85B - $650M) / $650M × 100 = 492%
```
