## ROI Measurement & Optimization

### Financial Metrics

#### Return on Investment (ROI)
- **Formula**: (Net Benefits - Total Investment) / Total Investment × 100
- **Calculation**: Annual benefits minus annual costs
- **Target**: 600% ROI in 8 months
- **Measurement**: Monthly tracking and reporting

#### Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)
- **Implementation Costs**: Technology, personnel, training
- **Operational Costs**: Ongoing maintenance and support
- **Hidden Costs**: Change management, process redesign
- **Total TCO**: Complete cost calculation

#### Payback Period
- **Calculation**: Total Investment / Annual Net Benefits
- **Target**: 1.9 months
