1. **Create Urgency**: Establish a sense of urgency
2. **Form Coalition**: Build a guiding coalition
3. **Create Vision**: Develop a change vision
4. **Communicate**: Communicate the vision
5. **Empower Action**: Remove obstacles and empower action
6. **Create Wins**: Generate short-term wins
7. **Build Momentum**: Consolidate gains and produce more change

- **System Integration Issues**: Incompatible systems
- **Data Quality Problems**: Inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Performance Issues**: System slowdowns or failures
- **Security Vulnerabilities**: Data breaches or unauthorized access
- **Vendor Dependencies**: Third-party service failures

#### Business Risks
- **Change Resistance**: Employee or customer resistance
- **Budget Overruns**: Exceeding planned costs
- **Timeline Delays**: Missing critical deadlines
- **Quality Issues**: Reduced service quality
- **Competitive Response**: Competitor reactions

#### Operational Risks
- **Resource Constraints**: Insufficient personnel or budget
- **Skill Gaps**: Lack of necessary expertise
