**Architecture Requirements:**
- Scalable compute resources
- Distributed data storage
- Real-time processing capabilities
- Security and compliance framework
- Observability and monitoring

**Technology Stack:**
```yaml
Compute: Kubernetes, GPU clusters
Storage: Data lakes, vector databases
Processing: Apache Spark, Ray
ML Platform: MLflow, Kubeflow
Monitoring: Prometheus, Grafana
```

**Data Pipeline Architecture:**
1. **Ingestion:** Multi-source data collection
2. **Storage:** Structured and unstructured data lakes
3. **Processing:** ETL/ELT workflows

## Talent & Organization

### Building the AI Team

**Core Roles:**

1. **Chief AI Officer (CAIO)**
   - Reports to CEO
   - Budget: $50M-$500M
   - Responsibilities: Strategy, governance, ROI

2. **AI Product Managers (5-10)**
   - Define use cases
   - Manage stakeholders
   - Measure outcomes

3. **ML Engineers (20-50)**
   - Build and deploy models
   - Optimize performance
   - Ensure reliability

4. **Data Engineers (15-30)**
   - Build data pipelines
   - Ensure data quality
   - Maintain infrastructure

5. **AI Ethics & Compliance (3-5)**
   - Governance framework
   - Risk assessment
   - Regulatory compliance

**Training Tracks:**

**Executive Track (2 days):**

**Technical Track (3 months):**

## Governance & Compliance

### AI Governance Framework

**Principles:**
1. **Transparency:** Explainable AI decisions
2. **Fairness:** Bias detection and mitigation
3. **Privacy:** Data protection by design
4. **Security:** Robust safeguards
5. **Accountability:** Clear ownership

### Ethical AI Guidelines

**Decision-Making Framework:**
```
Decision = (Technical Feasibility × Business Value × Ethical Soundness)
```

**Ethics Checklist:**
- [ ] Potential for bias assessed
- [ ] Privacy impact evaluated
- [ ] Security vulnerabilities identified
- [ ] Stakeholder impact analyzed
- [ ] Transparency requirements met
- [ ] Regulatory compliance verified

### Regulatory Compliance

**Key Regulations:**
- **GDPR:** Data protection (EU)
- **CCPA:** Consumer privacy (California)
- **AI Act:** AI regulation (EU)
- **HIPAA:** Healthcare data (US)
- **SOX:** Financial reporting (US)

**Compliance Program:**
- Regular audits
- Documentation requirements
- Training programs
- Incident response plans
