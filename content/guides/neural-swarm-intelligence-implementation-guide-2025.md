## Part 7: Common Pitfalls & Solutions

### Pitfall 1: Insufficient Executive Sponsorship
**Solution**: Build strong business case, demonstrate quick wins, maintain regular executive briefings.

### Pitfall 2: Underestimating Change Management
**Solution**: Allocate 30-40% of budget to change management, start early, involve users throughout.

### Pitfall 3: Poor Data Quality
**Solution**: Implement data quality framework, deploy validation agents, establish data governance.

### Pitfall 4: Inadequate Infrastructure
**Solution**: Right-size infrastructure from start, plan for growth, implement auto-scaling.

### Pitfall 5: Lack of Domain Expertise
**Solution**: Embed domain experts in team, implement knowledge capture, build communities of practice.
