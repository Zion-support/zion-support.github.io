## Frequently Asked Questions

### Will green AI reduce my model accuracy?

No. In fact, our optimizations often improve accuracy by forcing better model design. Our clients maintain 99.7% of original accuracy on average, with many seeing improvements.

### How quickly can we see results?

Quick wins like model optimization deliver results in 4-6 weeks. Full infrastructure transformation typically takes 6-12 months, but delivers benefits throughout the journey.

### What if we don't have sustainability goals yet?

We can help establish appropriate targets based on industry benchmarks, regulatory requirements, and shareholder expectations. Carbon neutrality is becoming table stakes for enterprise AI.

### Can we do this incrementally?

Absolutely. We design phased approaches that deliver value at each stage. Start with a pilot, prove ROI, then scale enterprise-wide.

### What about our existing AI infrastructure?

We optimize what you have before recommending new infrastructure. Many of our biggest wins come from software-level optimizations of existing systems.

### How do you measure carbon footprint?

We use comprehensive lifecycle assessment including energy consumption, hardware manufacturing, cooling infrastructure, and end-of-life disposal. We follow GHG Protocol and ISO 14064 standards.
