## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is neuromorphic computing?

Neuromorphic computing mimics the brain's neural architecture, achieving dramatic improvements in energy efficiency, speed, and adaptability compared to traditional AI systems.

### How is neuromorphic different from traditional AI?

Neuromorphic systems use spiking neural networks with integrated memory-processing, enabling real-time learning, 1000x better energy efficiency, and continuous adaptation without retraining.

### What are the best use cases for neuromorphic AI?

Ideal for real-time decision-making, edge computing, autonomous systems, video analytics, IoT applications, and any scenario requiring ultra-low latency or energy efficiency.

### What is the typical implementation timeline?

Pilots: 3-6 months | Production: 6-12 months | Enterprise: 12-24 months, depending on scope and complexity.

### What kind of ROI can we expect?

Clients typically achieve 500-20,000% ROI through cost savings (80-95% energy reduction) and operational improvements (25-45% efficiency gains).

### Do we need special hardware?

Yes, neuromorphic computing requires specialized processors (Intel Loihi, IBM TrueNorth, etc.), which we help select, procure, and integrate.

### What about existing AI investments?

Neuromorphic systems complement traditional AI through hybrid architectures, preserving existing investments while adding new capabilities.
