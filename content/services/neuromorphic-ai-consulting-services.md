## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is neuromorphic computing?
Neuromorphic computing mimics the neural structure of the human brain, using spiking neural networks (SNNs) that are event-driven and massively parallel, achieving extreme energy efficiency and low latency.

### How does it compare to traditional AI?
Neuromorphic AI is 1000x more energy-efficient, processes data in microseconds instead of milliseconds, and can learn continuously without retraining.
