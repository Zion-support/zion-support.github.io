**Impact**: 40-50% cost reduction with minimal quality impact

#### Fine-Tuning Programs

Create specialized models for your specific needs:

- **Custom Models**: Task-specific fine-tuned models
- **Training Pipeline**: Efficient data collection and training

**Impact**: 60-90% cost reduction for high-volume tasks

#### Hybrid Architectures

Combine traditional ML with LLMs intelligently:

- **Intent Classification**: Route queries appropriately
- **Template Responses**: Handle common queries efficiently
- **Selective LLM Usage**: Apply expensive models only when needed

**Impact**: 50-70% of queries handled by cheaper methods
