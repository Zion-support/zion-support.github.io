### Q: How long until we see ROI?
**A:** Most clients see measurable value within 90 days and full ROI within 12-18 months. Our fastest client achieved breakeven in 6 months.

### Q: What if our processes are unique?
**A:** We specialize in custom agent development. 80% of our agents are custom-built for client-specific workflows.

### Q: Can we start small and expand?
**A:** Absolutely! We recommend starting with a pilot (50-100 agents) and scaling based on proven results.

### Q: What happens if an agent makes a mistake?
**A:** Multiple safeguards: consensus mechanisms, human oversight for critical decisions, automatic rollback capabilities, and comprehensive audit logs.

### Q: Do we need special infrastructure?
**A:** We deploy on your existing cloud infrastructure (AWS, Azure, GCP) or can provide a fully managed solution.

**A:** RPA follows fixed rules; agent swarms exhibit emergent intelligence, adapt to changing conditions, and continuously improve through learning.

### Q: What about job displacement?
**A:** Our approach focuses on human-AI collaboration. Employees are upskilled for higher-value work. Most clients create new roles rather than eliminate existing ones.

### Q: Can we integrate with our existing AI/ML models?
**A:** Yes! Agent swarms can incorporate your existing models as specialized agents, creating a unified intelligence platform.
