## Our XAI Platform

### Comprehensive Explanation Methods

**Model-Agnostic Methods:**
- **SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations)**: Global feature importance
- **LIME (Local Interpretable Model-agnostic Explanations)**: Local decision explanations
- **Permutation Feature Importance**: Robust feature ranking
- **Partial Dependence Plots**: Feature relationship visualization

**Model-Specific Methods:**
- **Attention Visualization**: For transformer and neural network models
- **Decision Tree Extraction**: From complex ensemble models
- **Layer-wise Relevance Propagation**: Deep learning interpretability
- **Integrated Gradients**: Neural network attribution

**Advanced Explanations:**
- **Counterfactual Generation**: "What-if" scenario analysis
- **Causal Inference**: Moving beyond correlation to causation
- **Conceptual Explanations**: High-level decision factors
- **Natural Language Explanations**: Human-friendly descriptions

## FAQs

**Q: How long does implementation take?**
A: Typically 6-12 months for full enterprise deployment, with initial value in first 3 months.

**Q: What if our models are proprietary or complex?**
A: Our model-agnostic methods work with any AI system. We've successfully explained complex ensembles, deep neural networks, and proprietary algorithms.

A: Explanation generation is separate from inference. Real-time explanations are typically <100ms. Batch explanations have no impact on production systems.

**Q: Can we use our existing ML infrastructure?**
A: Yes, our platform integrates with existing MLOps tools, cloud platforms, and on-premise systems.

**Q: How do you handle sensitive data?**
