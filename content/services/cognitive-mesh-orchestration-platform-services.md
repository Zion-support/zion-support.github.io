## What is Cognitive Mesh Orchestration?

A Cognitive Mesh is a distributed network of AI agents that operate autonomously, coordinate seamlessly, adapt dynamically, and self-optimize through collective learning. Unlike traditional centralized AI systems, a cognitive mesh distributes intelligence across edge-to-cloud infrastructure for unprecedented scalability and resilience.
