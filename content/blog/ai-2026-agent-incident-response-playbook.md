# AI 2026: Agent Incident Response Playbook

Modern autonomous systems demand production-grade incident response. This playbook provides a practical guide for eval‑gated detection, mitigation, and escalation across agent platforms.

## What you will learn

- Incident taxonomy for autonomous agents
- Eval‑gated triage flows and rollback patterns
- On‑call rotations for agents‑on‑call
- Observability signals and SLOs for safety and reliability
- Post‑incident reviews and continuous hardening

## Core principles

1. Evaluation‑first operations: every risky action is gated by checks.
2. Policy‑as‑code governance for consistency and auditability.
3. Layered containment with sandboxes and time‑boxed permissions.
4. Human‑in‑the‑loop for irreversible or high‑risk operations.

## Quickstart

- Define incident severities and runbooks.
- Wire evaluation harnesses to critical actions.
- Instrument traces, evaluations, and safety telemetry.
- Practice game days to validate automation and escalation.

> Outcome: Faster MTTR, safer autonomy, and audit‑ready operations.
