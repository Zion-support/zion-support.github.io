## What is Quantum Computing?

Quantum computing harnesses the principles of quantum mechanics to process information in ways that classical computers cannot. Unlike traditional bits that exist in states of 0 or 1, quantum bits (qubits) can exist in multiple states simultaneously, enabling exponential computational power.
