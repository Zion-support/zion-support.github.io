## Common Pitfalls and How to Avoid Them

### 1. Lack of Executive Support
**Problem**: Insufficient C-suite commitment and resource allocation
**Solution**: Develop compelling business cases with clear ROI projections and secure executive sponsorship early

### 2. Poor Data Quality
**Problem**: Implementing AI on low-quality or incomplete data
**Solution**: Invest in data quality improvement and governance before AI implementation

### 3. Technology-First Approach
**Problem**: Focusing on technology rather than business outcomes
**Solution**: Start with business objectives and work backward to technology requirements

### 4. Insufficient Change Management
**Problem**: Resistance to change and lack of user adoption
**Solution**: Implement comprehensive change management and user training programs

### 5. Inadequate Security Measures
**Problem**: Security vulnerabilities and compliance issues
**Solution**: Implement security-by-design principles and comprehensive compliance frameworks
