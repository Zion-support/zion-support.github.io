## What Are Autonomous Business Operations?

Autonomous business operations represent the next evolution of business automation, where AI systems make independent decisions, execute complex workflows, and continuously optimize processes without human intervention. Unlike traditional automation, autonomous systems can:

- **Self-heal** when issues arise
- **Learn and adapt** to changing conditions
- **Make complex decisions** using real-time data
- **Scale dynamically** based on demand
- **Optimize continuously** for better outcomes
