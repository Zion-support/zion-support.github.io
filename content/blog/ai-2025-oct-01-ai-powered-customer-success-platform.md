**Real-Time Health Dashboard**:

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

❌ **Over-automation**: Losing human touch
✅ **Solution**: Use AI to empower humans, not replace them

❌ **Poor data quality**: Inaccurate predictions
✅ **Solution**: Invest in data integration and cleansing

❌ **Ignoring AI insights**: Not acting on recommendations
✅ **Solution**: Make AI insights actionable and trackable

❌ **Generic engagement**: One-size-fits-all approach
✅ **Solution**: Leverage AI for personalization

❌ **Lack of metrics**: Can't prove ROI
✅ **Solution**: Define and track KPIs from day one
