Enterprises are wiring autonomy behind policy. This blueprint shows how to encode approvals, risk tiers, evaluation gates, and runtime guardrails as versioned policy artifacts that integrate with CI/CD and production gateways.

Key components:

1. Policy schema: actions, resources, risk tiers, approvals, exemptions
2. Evidence adapters: eval results, incidents, releases, audits
3. Gateways: pre-merge, pre-deploy, pre-execute
4. Scorecards: safety, quality, reliability, cost

Outcomes: faster iteration, safer rollouts, and audit-ready autonomy.
