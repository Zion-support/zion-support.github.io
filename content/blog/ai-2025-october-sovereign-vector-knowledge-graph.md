# Sovereign Vector Knowledge Graph — Enterprise Blueprint

This guide shows how to architect a sovereign, air-gapped vector knowledge graph that powers compliant RAG, decision support, and autonomous workflows.

## Reference Architecture

- Hybrid embeddings + symbolic graph edges
- Content provenance, lineage, and cryptographic attestations
- Zero egress controls; per-tenant sharding

## SLO Targets

- 99.99% read availability, 99.9% write availability
- <100ms P95 retrieval across 100M+ items

## Compliance

Meets regional residency, audit, and redaction requirements with end-to-end policy enforcement and explainability.
