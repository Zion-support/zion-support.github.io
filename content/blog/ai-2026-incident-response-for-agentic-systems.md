Agentic systems introduce new classes of failure. This playbook focuses on observability-driven incident response to lower MTTR while maintaining safety and compliance.

Highlights:

- Safety reviews and red-teaming prior to launch
- Real-time traces for prompts, tool invocations, and policy checks
- Escalation paths with human-in-the-loop approvals
- Evidence capture and signed bundles for audits

Drill template:

1. Define scenario and expected safeguards
2. Run through canary environment with live evals
3. Capture telemetry, confirm abort switches
4. Document findings and add new checks

Operational excellence emerges from repeatable drills and measurable guardrails.
