This blueprint details a modular evaluation harness:

- Dataset loaders and scenario generators
- Metric calculators and quality thresholds
- Policy gates for CI/CD release decisions
- Reporters for dashboards and postmortems

Use it to gate agent releases and continuously improve reliability and safety.
