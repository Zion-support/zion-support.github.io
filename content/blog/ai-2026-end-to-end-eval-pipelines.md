# AI 2026: End-to-End Evaluation Pipelines

Shipping reliable autonomy requires evaluation-first workflows. This guide covers designing, implementing, and operating end-to-end evaluation pipelines across the ML and agent lifecycle.

## Highlights

- Evaluation harnesses for critical actions
- Golden datasets and shadow traffic
- Policy-as-code gates and rollbacks
- Observability, scoring, and release criteria

## Why it matters

Evaluation pipelines reduce risk, speed up iteration, and enable auditable decisions. With clear thresholds and safety checks, teams can confidently scale autonomous systems.
