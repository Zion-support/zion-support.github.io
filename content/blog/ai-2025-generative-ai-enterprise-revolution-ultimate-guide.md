## ROI Calculation and Measurement

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
1. **Operational Efficiency**
   - Process automation percentage
   - Time-to-completion reduction
   - Error rate improvement
   - Resource utilization optimization

2. **Financial Impact**
   - Cost savings achieved
   - Revenue generation increase
   - ROI percentage
   - Payback period

3. **User Experience**
   - User satisfaction scores
   - Adoption rates
   - Productivity improvements
   - Innovation metrics

### ROI Calculation Formula
```
ROI = (Net Benefits - Total Investment) / Total Investment × 100

Where:
Net Benefits = Cost Savings + Revenue Increase + Productivity Gains
Total Investment = Technology + Implementation + Training + Maintenance
```
