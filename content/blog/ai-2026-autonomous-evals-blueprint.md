Enterprises are shifting to an evaluation-first operating model for agentic systems. This blueprint covers:

- Scenario suites tied to user journeys and safety constraints
- Quantitative pass thresholds and red-team coverage
- CI/CD regression gates and rollout checks
- Runtime drift monitors and incident feedback loops

Adopt layered gates to reduce regressions, raise reliability, and align behavior with business KPIs.
