## Implementation Best Practices

### 1. Data Quality and Governance

#### Data Quality Framework
```yaml
Data Quality Standards:
  - Completeness: >95% for critical fields
  - Accuracy: >99% for financial data
  - Consistency: Cross-system validation
  - Timeliness: Real-time for operational data
  - Validity: Schema compliance and business rules
```

- **Data Stewardship**

  - Privacy and compliance oversight
