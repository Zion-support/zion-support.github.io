## The Edge-Native Paradigm Shift

### Traditional vs. Edge-Native AI

Traditional cloud-centric AI architectures suffer from:
- High latency due to round-trip communication
- Single points of failure
- Bandwidth bottlenecks
- Privacy and compliance challenges
- Unpredictable costs

Edge-native autonomous intelligence eliminates these limitations by:
- Processing data locally at the edge
- Autonomous decision-making without cloud dependency
- Distributed resilience and redundancy
- Privacy-first architecture
- Predictable, optimized costs
