# The Neural Interface Revolution: Transforming Human-AI Collaboration in 2025

## What Are Neural Interfaces?

### Definition and Technology
Neural interfaces, also known as brain-computer interfaces (BCIs), are direct communication pathways between the human brain and external devices. These systems can:
- **Read Brain Signals**: Interpret neural activity to understand thoughts and intentions
- **Stimulate Neural Activity**: Provide feedback directly to the brain
- **Enable Direct Control**: Allow thought-based control of devices and systems

### Types of Neural Interfaces
- **Invasive**: Implanted directly into brain tissue for maximum precision
- **Non-invasive**: External devices that read brain signals through the skull
- **Hybrid**: Combining multiple approaches for optimal performance
