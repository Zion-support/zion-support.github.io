# Enterprise AI Roadmap 2025: Scorecards, Guardrails, and Reliable Velocity

Enterprises are moving beyond pilots to production AI that must be reliable, governable, and cost‑predictable. This guide lays out a pragmatic roadmap grounded in KPI‑linked scorecards, guardrails as code, and rollback‑first operations that keep teams shipping fast without regressions.

## 1) Fund Outcomes, Not Experiments
- Define business‑level KPIs and map them to leading‑indicator SLIs
- Tie budgets to guardrails: error budgets, token budgets, latency budgets
- Review weekly with a living scorecard that drives decisions

## 2) Guardrails as Code
- Intent verification, deterministic tool contracts, and reversible actions
- Policy checks in CI and at runtime; evaluator hooks as deploy gates
- Canary playbooks with automatic rollback on KPI regression

## 3) Stable Cost at Scale
- Quality tiers + semantic/workflow caches for predictable spend
- Budget‑aware routing with graceful degradation and fallbacks
- Continuous profiling and cache freshness windows

## 4) Operating Model
- Approval tiers for risky actions, human‑in‑the‑loop escalation
- Incident‑ready pipelines: mirrors, automated canaries, one‑click rollback
- Weekly rituals: scorecard review, post‑incident learnings, policy updates
