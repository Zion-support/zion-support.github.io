## The Multi-Model Imperative

### Why Single Models Fail at Scale

Organizations that rely on a single LLM face critical limitations:

1. **Cost inefficiency**: Using GPT-4 for simple tasks wastes 85% of budget
2. **Performance gaps**: No single model excels at all tasks
3. **Vendor lock-in**: Dependence on one provider creates risk
4. **Compliance issues**: Data residency and privacy concerns
5. **Scalability challenges**: Single provider bandwidth limits
