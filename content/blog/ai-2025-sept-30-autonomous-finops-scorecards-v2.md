# Autonomous FinOps Scorecards (v2) — Prevent Cost Regressions

Implement scorecards that connect spend to outcomes:

- Track cost, latency, and quality per workflow
- Route by tier; cache aggressively for hot paths
- Quantize, batch, and prune to stabilize spend
- Roll back instantly when KPIs regress

Impact: 35–55% cost reduction with unchanged UX.
