## What is Neural Mesh Computing?

NMC creates a self-organizing network of AI processing nodes that:

1. **Dynamically Distribute Intelligence**: Workloads automatically route to optimal processing locations
2. **Self-Heal and Self-Optimize**: Network adapts to failures and changing conditions
3. **Scale Infinitely**: Add nodes without architectural changes
4. **Operate Autonomously**: No central control or single point of failure
5. **Learn Collectively**: Federated learning across the entire mesh
