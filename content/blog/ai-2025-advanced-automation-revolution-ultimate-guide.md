## ROI Calculation Framework

### Direct Cost Savings

#### 1. Labor Cost Reduction
- **Formula**: (Hours Automated × Hourly Rate × 12 months) × Efficiency Multiplier
- **Example**: 40 hours/week × $50/hour × 12 months × 0.85 efficiency = $20,400/year

#### 2. Error Reduction Savings
- **Formula**: (Error Rate Reduction × Cost per Error × Frequency) × 12 months
- **Example**: 0.15 × $500 × 100 errors/month × 12 months = $90,000/year

#### 3. Process Speed Improvement
- **Formula**: (Time Saved × Value per Hour × Frequency) × 12 months
- **Example**: 2 hours × $100/hour × 50 processes/month × 12 months = $120,000/year

### 1. Underestimating Change Management

**Problem**: Employees resist automation changes
**Solution**: Invest heavily in training and communication

**Problem**: Automating low-value, complex processes
**Solution**: Start with high-value, simple processes
