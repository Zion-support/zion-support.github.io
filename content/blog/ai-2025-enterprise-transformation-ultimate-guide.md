## ROI Measurement Framework

### Financial Metrics
- **Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)**: Implementation and operational costs
- **Return on Investment (ROI)**: Net benefits divided by total costs
- **Payback Period**: Time to recover initial investment
- **Net Present Value (NPV)**: Discounted future cash flows

### Operational Metrics
- **Process Efficiency**: Time and resource savings
- **Quality Improvement**: Error reduction and accuracy gains
- **Customer Satisfaction**: Net Promoter Score and retention
- **Employee Productivity**: Output per employee

### Strategic Metrics
- **Market Position**: Competitive advantage gains
- **Innovation Rate**: New product and service development
- **Digital Maturity**: AI adoption across functions
- **Future Readiness**: Scalability and adaptability
