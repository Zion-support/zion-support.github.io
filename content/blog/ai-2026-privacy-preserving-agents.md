# AI 2026: Privacy-Preserving Agents

Build agents that protect sensitive data without sacrificing utility:

- Scoped memory with timeboxed retention and PII classifiers
- Structured redaction and reversible tokenization
- Enclave-backed tool use and confidential computing
- Evidence capture for audits and approvals

Outcomes: lower breach risk, faster approvals, continuous compliance.
