Autonomous business systems are AI-powered solutions that operate independently, making decisions and executing processes without human intervention. These systems combine:

- **Machine Learning**: Continuous learning and adaptation
- **Natural Language Processing**: Understanding and generating human language
- **Computer Vision**: Visual recognition and analysis
- **Robotic Process Automation**: Automated task execution
- **Predictive Analytics**: Future-focused decision making
