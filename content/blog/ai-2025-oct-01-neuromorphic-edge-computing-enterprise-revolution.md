## What is Neuromorphic Edge Computing?

Neuromorphic computing mimics the neural structure and functioning of the human brain, using spiking neural networks (SNNs) instead of traditional artificial neural networks. When combined with edge deployment, this creates a powerful platform for:

### Core Breakthrough Capabilities

1. **Event-Driven Processing**: Only processes data when events occur, dramatically reducing power consumption
2. **Continuous Learning**: Adapts and learns from new data without retraining
3. **Massively Parallel Architecture**: Processes thousands of inputs simultaneously
4. **Ultra-Low Latency**: Makes decisions in microseconds, not milliseconds
