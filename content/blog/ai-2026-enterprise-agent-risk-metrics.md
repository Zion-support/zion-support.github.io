# AI 2026: Enterprise Agent Risk Metrics — Measuring Safety, Reliability, and ROI

Modern autonomous agents introduce new operational risks. This guide provides a concise, actionable framework for defining leading and lagging indicators, wiring them into evaluation gates, and reporting outcomes to business stakeholders.

## Core metrics

- Safety violations per 1k actions (SV/1k)
- Policy-as-code gate pass rate (PaC-PR)
- Production incident rate (PIR)
- Mean time to mitigate (MTTM)
- Customer-impacting regressions (CIR)
- Guarded tool misuse rate (GTMR)
- Cost per successful task (CPST)

## Lifecycle gates

Integrate metrics at pre-deploy, canary, and full rollout. Block promotion when thresholds breach. Persist evidence for audit.

## Dashboard and reporting

Expose SLOs, gate outcomes, and drift alerts. Tie savings and ROI to risk posture improvements.

## Implementation checklist

- Define thresholds per risk tier
- Instrument actions and tools
- Record evaluations and attach artifacts
- Automate rollbacks on breach
- Review metrics weekly with owners
