## Common Pitfalls & How to Avoid Them

### 1. Over-Automation
**Pitfall**: Automating processes that shouldn't be automated
**Avoidance**: Carefully evaluate automation suitability
**Solution**: Focus on high-value, repetitive processes

### 2. Insufficient Testing
**Pitfall**: Deploying autonomous systems without adequate testing
**Avoidance**: Implement comprehensive testing and validation
**Solution**: Use sandbox environments and gradual rollout

### 3. Poor Change Management
**Pitfall**: Resistance to autonomous systems
**Avoidance**: Invest in change management and training
**Solution**: Comprehensive communication and support

### 4. Inadequate Monitoring
**Pitfall**: Deploying autonomous systems without monitoring
**Avoidance**: Implement comprehensive monitoring and alerting

### 5. Lack of Governance
