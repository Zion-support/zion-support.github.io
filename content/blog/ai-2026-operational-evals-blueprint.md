# AI 2026: Operational Evals Blueprint — Ship Faster with Guarded Quality

Enterprises are moving from offline, ad hoc evaluation to continuous, operational evals. This blueprint shows how to wire traces, scenarios, metrics, and policy gates into your release process so every change ships with evidence.

## What you will learn

- Why operational evals reduce change failure rate and rollback time
- How to model journey scenarios and attach quality metrics
- How to enforce gates in CI/CD and feature flags
- How to build telemetry for audit-ready evidence

## Architecture

1. Data collection via traces and prompts
2. Scenario suites and metrics library
3. Policy-as-code gates
4. Live dashboards and incident triggers

## Outcomes

- Gate pass rate > 95%
- Rollback time < 5 minutes
- Audit-ready artifacts by default
