## Best Practices

### Start Small, Scale Fast
1. Begin with non-critical workloads
2. Build confidence through successful automation
3. Expand gradually to mission-critical systems
4. Maintain human oversight during initial phases

### Data-Driven Decision Making
- Collect comprehensive telemetry data
- Train ML models on 12+ months of historical data
- Validate predictions before automated actions
- Measure and report on success metrics

### Safety and Governance
- Implement circuit breakers for automation
- Maintain audit trails for all automated actions
- Define clear escalation paths
- Regular review and refinement of automation policies
