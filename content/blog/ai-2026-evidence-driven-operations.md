Modern AI operations must be evidence-driven: every change, rollout, and escalation backed by reproducible evaluations and audit-ready artifacts.

Key pillars:

- Evaluation-first: golden sets, canaries, and live evals gate changes
- Policy-as-code: executable guardrails with approvals and rollback hooks
- Observability: traces, metrics, and safety telemetry across the lifecycle
- SLOs that matter: quality, safety, latency, reliability, and cost

Starter playbook:

1. Define SLOs and failure budgets for agent tasks
2. Standardize eval harness and artifacts schema
3. Enforce PaC gates in CI/CD and progressive delivery
4. Instrument traces and decision logs for each action/tool
5. Review weekly scorecards; tune prompts, tools, and policies

Outcomes: faster safe releases, fewer incidents, and measurable ROI.
