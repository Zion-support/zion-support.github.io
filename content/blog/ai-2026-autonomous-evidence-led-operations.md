# AI 2026: Autonomous Evidence‑Led Operations — Centralizing Evals, Incidents, and KPIs

Evidence‑led operations unify evaluations, incidents, approvals, and KPIs in a single hub. Benefits include:

- Faster approvals with policy‑as‑code and standardized evidence
- Consistent guardrails across environments with audit‑ready history
- Improved reliability via gates, SLO scorecards, and automated reports

Stand up an “Evidence Hub” that ingests eval results, traces, prompts, incidents, and rollout states, and expose dashboards for release readiness and risk posture.

Adopt eval‑gated SOPs, map SLOs to business outcomes, and link incidents to scenario coverage to drive continuous improvement.

# AI 2026: Autonomous, Evidence‑Led Operations

Evidence is the currency of trust. Run AI platforms with gated changes, observable behavior, and auditable decisions.

## Core Practices

- Eval‑gated CI/CD and staged promotion
- Golden signals for agents: quality, safety, cost, latency
- Evidence hubs: traces, evals, incidents, approvals
- SLOs for agent platforms and scenario coverage

## Practical Steps

1. Define SLOs and scenario suites per capability
2. Add pre‑merge evals and pre‑prod smoke suites
3. Ship with progressive delivery and auto‑rollback
4. Centralize evidence for audits and reviews
