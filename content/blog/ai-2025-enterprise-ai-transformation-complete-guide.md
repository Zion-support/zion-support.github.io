## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

### 1. Technology-First Approach
- **Problem**: Focusing on technology before business needs
- **Solution**: Start with business objectives and work backward

### 2. Insufficient Data Preparation
- **Problem**: Poor data quality leading to poor AI performance
- **Solution**: Invest heavily in data quality and governance

### 3. Lack of Executive Support
- **Problem**: AI initiatives failing due to lack of leadership support
- **Solution**: Secure strong executive sponsorship and governance

### 4. Ignoring Change Management
- **Problem**: Resistance to AI adoption from employees
- **Solution**: Comprehensive change management and training programs

### 5. Overlooking Security and Compliance
- **Problem**: AI systems vulnerable to attacks and non-compliant
- **Solution**: Security-first design and compliance framework
