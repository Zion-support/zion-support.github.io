# AI Innovation Playbook 2025

AI success in 2025 requires disciplined execution across strategy, data, and delivery. This playbook outlines a practical path:

## 1. Strategy And Use-Case Triage
- Tie initiatives to measurable OKRs
- Prioritize feasibility: data readiness, model availability, risk

## 2. Data Foundation
- Establish quality gates, lineage, and PII governance
- Adopt feature stores to accelerate reuse

## 3. Delivery And MLOps
- Standardize CICD for models, tests, and rollback
- Implement continuous monitoring for drift and bias

## 4. Risk And Compliance
- Maintain audit trails and model cards
- Add human-in-the-loop for high-risk decisions

## 5. Scale And Change Management
- Create a central enablement team
- Upskill squads with platform tooling and guardrails
