A practical blueprint for securing AI systems end-to-end:

- Model security: prompt injection hardening, output validation, red-teaming
- Data security: PII detection, masking, differential privacy where applicable
- Runtime: sandboxed tool use, policy-as-code, guardrails with deny-by-default
- Supply chain: signed artifacts, SBOM, model provenance, dependency scanning
- Monitoring: eval-gated releases, safety telemetry, anomaly detection

Tie controls to measurable SLIs/SLOs and enforce via your CI/CD.
