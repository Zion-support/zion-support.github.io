# AI 2026: Autonomous Ops Roadmap — Shipping Reliable Agents at Scale

Enterprises are moving from agent pilots to production. This roadmap outlines the phases, controls, and telemetry you need to deliver safe, observable, and cost‑efficient autonomy.

## Phase 1 — Foundation (0‑2 months)
- Define platform SLOs for safety, quality, reliability, and economics
- Stand up evaluation harness with representative scenarios and metrics
- Instrument traces, tokens, and policy decisions

## Phase 2 — Guardrails (2‑4 months)
- Policy‑as‑code for tool use, PII handling, and escalation
- Eval‑gated releases with canaries and auto‑rollback
- Incident triage runbooks and on‑call rotations

## Phase 3 — Scale (4‑8 months)
- Cost‑aware model routing and semantic caching
- Automated drift detection and weekly regression suites
- Tiered trust model and isolation domains for risky tools

## Phase 4 — Optimization (ongoing)
- Continuous scenario expansion from incidents and feedback
- ROI reporting with cost per task and value captured
- Change management integrated with evidence vault

## Starter metrics
- Eval pass rate > 95%
- Incident MTTR < 30 min
- Cost per task trending down QoQ
