Enterprises running autonomous agents need repeatable, auditable, and measurable releases. This checklist distills best practices:

1. Define pass/fail criteria mapped to SLOs and risk tiers.
2. Run pre‑merge and pre‑deploy eval suites; block on regressions.
3. Use progressive delivery with live eval probes and automatic rollback.
4. Capture evidence: eval IDs, policy bundle versions, change tickets, and approvals.
5. Monitor error budgets, latency, cost, and safety incidents post‑release.

Starter artifacts include a CI template, policy stubs, and an evidence schema you can adopt today.
