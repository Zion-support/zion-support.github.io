   - Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)
   - Revenue per Employee (RPE)
   - Cost per Transaction (CPT)

2. **Operational Metrics**
   - Process Efficiency Improvement
   - Automation Rate
   - Error Reduction
   - Time to Market

3. **Customer Metrics**
   - Customer Satisfaction Score
   - Net Promoter Score (NPS)
   - Customer Retention Rate
   - Response Time

### ROI Calculation Framework

**Formula**: ROI = (Net Benefits - Total Investment) / Total Investment × 100%

**Net Benefits Include:**
- Cost savings from automation
- Revenue increases from new capabilities
- Efficiency gains and productivity improvements
- Risk reduction and compliance benefits

**Total Investment Includes:**
- Technology licensing and infrastructure
- Implementation and integration costs
- Training and change management
- Ongoing maintenance and support
