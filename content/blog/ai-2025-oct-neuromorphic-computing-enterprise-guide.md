## Understanding Neuromorphic Computing

### What Makes Neuromorphic Different?

**Traditional Computing vs. Neuromorphic:**

| Aspect | Traditional AI | Neuromorphic AI |
|--------|---------------|-----------------|
| Architecture | Von Neumann (separate memory/processing) | Integrated memory-processing (like brain) |
| Energy Efficiency | High power consumption | 1000x more efficient |
| Learning | Batch training, static models | Continuous online learning |
| Latency | Milliseconds to seconds | Microseconds (real-time) |
| Adaptability | Requires retraining | Self-adapting in real-time |

### Core Principles

1. **Spiking Neural Networks (SNNs)**
   - Event-driven processing
   - Temporal dynamics
   - Energy-efficient computation

2. **In-Memory Computing**
   - Co-located processing and memory
   - Reduced data movement
   - Ultra-low latency

3. **Continuous Learning**
   - Online adaptation
   - No retraining required
   - Real-world learning
