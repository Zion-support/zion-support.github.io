Autonomous marketplaces are emerging where agents negotiate, contract, and settle with safety rails. This blueprint outlines architecture, guardrails, and rollout steps to operate sovereign AI commerce responsibly.

## Architecture

1. Identity and permissions with verifiable credentials
2. Sandboxed tool use with policy-as-code enforcement
3. Continuous evaluations and incident-ready telemetry

## Rollout checklist

- Define risk tiers and allowed actions
- Canary releases with auto rollback
- Incident playbooks with on-call agents

## Outcomes

- Trustworthy transactions with audit trails
- Faster settlement cycles and lower operating costs
