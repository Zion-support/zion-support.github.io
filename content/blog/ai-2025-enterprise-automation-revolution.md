## ROI Calculation Framework

### Direct Cost Savings

1. **Labor Cost Reduction**
   - Hours saved per process × hourly rate × frequency
   - Example: 40 hours/week × $50/hour × 52 weeks = $104,000/year

2. **Error Reduction Costs**
   - Error rate reduction × cost per error × volume
   - Example: 50% error reduction × $500/error × 100 errors = $25,000/year

3. **Process Efficiency Gains**
   - Time savings × opportunity cost
   - Example: 30% faster processing × $100,000 value = $30,000/year

### Indirect Benefits

1. **Improved Customer Satisfaction**
   - Higher retention rates and increased lifetime value
   - Reduced churn costs and increased referrals

2. **Enhanced Employee Productivity**
   - Focus on high-value tasks instead of manual processes
   - Improved job satisfaction and retention

3. **Competitive Advantage**
   - Faster response times and better service quality
   - Ability to scale without proportional cost increases
