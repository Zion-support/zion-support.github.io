### 2. Ignoring Change Management
**Problem**: Not preparing teams for transformation
**Solution**: Invest in comprehensive training and communication

### 3. Poor Data Quality
**Problem**: AI systems trained on incomplete or inaccurate data
**Solution**: Implement data governance and quality controls

### 4. Lack of Monitoring
**Problem**: Deploying automation without performance tracking
**Solution**: Establish comprehensive monitoring and alerting systems
