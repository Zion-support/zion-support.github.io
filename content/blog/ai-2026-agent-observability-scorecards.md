Modern agent platforms generate rich signals but lack synthesis. This article outlines a practical approach to unify:

- Core traces and span annotations for tool use
- Live evaluation gates for safety and quality
- Policy and approval events from governance workflows

The outcome is a single scorecard per capability that leaders can review weekly. Start with 3 KPIs: safety violations, successful task completion, and cost per successful task. Wire the scorecard to deployment policy and incident runbooks so action follows insight.
