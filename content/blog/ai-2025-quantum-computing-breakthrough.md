## The Quantum-AI Revolution

Quantum computing has finally reached the threshold where practical business applications become viable. Unlike classical computers that process information in binary bits (0s and 1s), quantum computers use quantum bits (qubits) that can exist in multiple states simultaneously. This quantum superposition, combined with entanglement, enables exponential computational power that's revolutionizing AI capabilities.

**Quantum Decoherence**: Quantum systems are sensitive to environmental interference

- **Solution**: Use hybrid quantum-classical approaches and cloud-based quantum services

**Integration Complexity**: Integrating quantum systems with existing infrastructure
