## What is Quantum Computing?

Quantum computing leverages the principles of quantum mechanics to process information in ways that are impossible for classical computers:

### Quantum Principles
- **Superposition**: Qubits can exist in multiple states simultaneously
- **Entanglement**: Qubits can be instantaneously connected across any distance
- **Interference**: Quantum states can amplify or cancel each other out
- **Tunneling**: Quantum particles can pass through energy barriers

### Quantum vs Classical Computing
- **Classical**: Bits are either 0 or 1
- **Quantum**: Qubits can be 0, 1, or any superposition of both
- **Processing Power**: Exponential increase in computational capacity
- **Problem Solving**: Can solve problems that would take classical computers billions of years
