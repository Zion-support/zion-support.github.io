# AI 2026: Operational Reliability Blueprint

Reliable autonomy requires objective evidence across the delivery lifecycle. This blueprint outlines:

- Evaluation gates in CI/CD and runtime
- Release scorecards with SLOs for safety, quality, reliability, and cost
- Deterministic rollback contracts and approval workflows

Key outcomes:

- Gate pass rate > 95%
- Change failure rate < 3%
- Rollback time < 5 min
