## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

### 1. Underestimating Change Management
- **Problem**: Resistance to change and low adoption rates
- **Solution**: Invest heavily in training and communication

### 2. Choosing Wrong Processes
- **Problem**: Automating processes that don't provide clear value
- **Solution**: Conduct thorough process analysis and ROI assessment

### 3. Insufficient Testing
- **Problem**: Deploying solutions that don't work as expected
- **Solution**: Implement comprehensive testing and pilot programs

### 4. Lack of Executive Support
- **Problem**: Projects fail due to lack of resources and support
- **Solution**: Secure strong executive sponsorship from the start

### 5. Poor Integration Planning
- **Problem**: Solutions that don't work well with existing systems
- **Solution**: Plan integration carefully and test thoroughly
