# Case Study: 65% Faster Support With AI Agents

We partnered with a fintech to deploy retrieval-augmented agents across email and chat. Outcomes:

- Median first-response time: 7m → 2m
- Resolution automation rate: 0% → 42%
- CSAT: 4.1 → 4.6

Key enablers: grounded retrieval, safe action tools, and robust escalation.
