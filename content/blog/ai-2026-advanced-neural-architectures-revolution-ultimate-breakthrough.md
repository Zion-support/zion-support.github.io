#### 2. Residual Connections
- **Purpose**: Enable training of deeper networks without degradation
- **Benefits**: 500% improvement in gradient flow
- **Applications**: Image recognition, complex pattern matching

#### 3. Skip Connections
- **Purpose**: Preserve information flow across network layers
- **Benefits**: 400% improvement in information retention
- **Applications**: Data reconstruction, anomaly detection

#### 4. Dynamic Routing
- **Purpose**: Adaptive information flow based on input characteristics
- **Benefits**: 600% improvement in computational efficiency
- **Applications**: Real-time decision making, resource allocation

### Performance Optimization Techniques

#### 1. Quantization
- **Method**: Reduce precision of model parameters
- **Benefits**: 75% reduction in memory usage
- **Impact**: 3x faster inference without accuracy loss

#### 2. Pruning
- **Method**: Remove unnecessary connections and parameters
- **Benefits**: 60% reduction in model size
- **Impact**: 2.5x faster training and inference

#### 3. Knowledge Distillation
- **Method**: Transfer knowledge from large models to smaller ones
- **Benefits**: 80% reduction in computational requirements
- **Impact**: Maintain performance with 5x less resources
