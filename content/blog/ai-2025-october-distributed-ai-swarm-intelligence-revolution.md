## What is Swarm Intelligence?

Swarm intelligence (SI) applies principles observed in nature—bee colonies, ant colonies, bird flocks—to artificial intelligence systems. In enterprise contexts, this means:

- **Decentralized Decision-Making**: No single point of failure
- **Emergent Behavior**: Collective intelligence exceeds individual agent capabilities
- **Self-Organization**: Automatic adaptation to changing conditions
- **Scalability**: Linear scaling from hundreds to millions of agents
