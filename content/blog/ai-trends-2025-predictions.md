## 6. Edge AI Revolution

### Computing at the Source
Moving AI processing to the edge of networks, closer to where data is generated and decisions need to be made.

### Key Benefits
- **Ultra-Low Latency**: Sub-millisecond response times
- **Privacy Preservation**: Data processing without cloud transmission
- **Bandwidth Efficiency**: Reduced data transmission requirements
- **Offline Capability**: AI functionality without internet connectivity

### Business Applications
- **IoT Devices**: Smart sensors with built-in AI
- **Autonomous Vehicles**: Real-time decision-making without cloud dependency
- **Industrial Automation**: Edge-based quality control and optimization
- **Retail**: In-store AI for personalized customer experiences
