## The Edge Computing Revolution

Edge computing brings computation closer to data sources, enabling:

- **Real-time Processing**: Immediate data analysis and decision-making
- **Reduced Latency**: 10-100x faster response times
- **Enhanced Privacy**: Data processing without cloud transmission
- **Bandwidth Optimization**: Reduced data transmission costs
