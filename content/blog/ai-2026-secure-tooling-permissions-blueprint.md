# AI 2026: Secure Tooling Permissions — Blueprint

Control how agents act in production environments:

- Risk-tiered tool adapters with scoped capabilities
- Policy-as-code for approvals, rate limits, and evidence requirements
- Just-in-time elevation with expiry and audit trails
- Kill switches, budgets, and environment guards

Outcome: safer automation with clear boundaries and rapid recovery.
