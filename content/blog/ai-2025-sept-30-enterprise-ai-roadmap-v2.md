# Enterprise AI Roadmap 2025 — Pragmatic Guide (v2)

This guide outlines a practical sequence to launch enterprise AI programs with measurable outcomes:

- Define KPI‑linked scorecards and guardrails
- Establish cost tiers, routing, and cache policies
- Add end‑to‑end traces and policy approvals
- Ship the first 3 production workflows in 90 days

Outcome: predictable delivery, stable UX, and 30‑60% cost reduction.
