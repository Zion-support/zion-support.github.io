This blueprint shows how to implement defensible, auditable billing controls for agentic systems:

- Policy‑as‑code budgets with alerts and hard stops
- Approval workflows integrated with release evidence
- Cost SLOs on critical journeys; route to cheaper models when safe
- Unit economics instrumentation and dashboards

Outcome: fewer surprise bills, faster approvals, and predictable scaling.
