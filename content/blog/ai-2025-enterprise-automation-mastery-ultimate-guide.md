- **Implementation Services**: $100,000 - $1,000,000

### ROI Calculation Framework

```
ROI = (Total Benefits - Total Costs) / Total Costs × 100

Where:
Total Benefits = Direct Savings + Indirect Benefits + Risk Mitigation
Total Costs = Initial Investment + Ongoing Costs + Opportunity Costs
```

- **6 months**: 15-25% ROI
- **12 months**: 40-80% ROI
- **24 months**: 100-300% ROI
