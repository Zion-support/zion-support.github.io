# Enterprise Agent Systems: Achieving 99.9% Uptime with Safe Autonomy

This guide outlines a reliability stack for agents operating in regulated, high‑stakes environments.

## Reliability Stack

- Capability‑scoped tools and permissions
- Action validators and formal constraints
- Guardrails with red‑team tests and kill‑switches
- Tracing with semantic spans and outcome labels
- Shadow, canary, and staged rollout patterns

## SLOs and Runbooks

- SLO: ≥ 99.9% task completion without intervention
- Error budget policies aligned to business criticality
- Runbooks for degraded modes and auto‑rollback
