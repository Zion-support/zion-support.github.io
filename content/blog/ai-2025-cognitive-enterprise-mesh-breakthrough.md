## What is Cognitive Enterprise Mesh?

Cognitive Enterprise Mesh is a revolutionary architecture that combines:

### 1. Intelligent Integration Layer
Self-configuring connectors that understand data structures, business logic, and system behaviors without manual programming.

### 2. Cognitive Orchestration
AI agents that coordinate workflows, manage dependencies, and optimize processes across your entire technology stack.

### 3. Adaptive Data Fabric
A living data layer that automatically maps, transforms, and routes information where it needs to go.

Machine learning systems that improve performance, efficiency, and capabilities over time.
