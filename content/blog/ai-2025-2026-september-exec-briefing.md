Leaders are asking: how do we move from pilots to reliable, governed autonomy? This briefing distills the minimum viable practices to scale safely.

## Five moves to make now

1. Define agent SLOs that include safety, quality, reliability, and cost
2. Gate autonomy with evaluation harnesses and policy-as-code checks
3. Instrument deep observability: traces, eval IDs, policy decisions
4. Standardize change management and incident runbooks for agents
5. Close the loop with risk metrics and post-incident learning

## Proof points

- 30–60% reduction in production regressions after adding eval gates
- 15–35% compute savings via trustworthy model routing
- Faster time-to-approval with standardized controls and evidence

The shift is operational. Treat agents like services with real SLOs, and make evidence the interface between teams.
