## Security, Privacy & Ethics

### Privacy-First Design

**Data Handling Principles:**
1. **User Control**: Employees control their personal data
2. **Transparency**: Clear explanation of what AI sees and learns
3. **Opt-In**: Voluntary participation in advanced features
4. **Privacy by Default**: Minimum necessary data collection
5. **Right to Delete**: Complete data removal on request

**Technical Implementation:**
- End-to-end encryption for all communications
- On-device processing where possible
- Federated learning for model training
- Differential privacy for aggregate analytics
- Regular security audits and penetration testing

### Ethical AI Framework

**Guiding Principles:**
1. **Augmentation, Not Replacement**: AI enhances human capabilities
2. **Transparency**: Explainable AI decisions
3. **Fairness**: Regular bias audits and corrections
4. **Accountability**: Human oversight of critical decisions

**Governance:**
