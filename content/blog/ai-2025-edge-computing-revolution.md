## What Is Edge Computing?

Edge computing is a distributed computing paradigm that brings computation and data storage closer to the sources of data. This approach:

### 1. Reduces Latency
- Processes data locally instead of sending to cloud
- Enables real-time decision making
- Improves user experience and system responsiveness

### 2. Enhances Privacy
- Keeps sensitive data on local devices
- Reduces data transmission over networks
- Complies with privacy regulations

### 3. Improves Reliability
- Works offline when needed
- Reduces dependency on network connectivity
- Provides better fault tolerance
