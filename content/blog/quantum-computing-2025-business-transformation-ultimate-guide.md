## The Quantum Advantage

### What Makes Quantum Computing Revolutionary?

Quantum computing leverages the principles of quantum mechanics to process information in ways that classical computers cannot:

- **Quantum Superposition**: Processing multiple states simultaneously
- **Quantum Entanglement**: Instantaneous correlation between particles
- **Quantum Interference**: Amplifying correct answers while canceling wrong ones
- **Quantum Tunneling**: Overcoming computational barriers
