# AI 2026: Go-To-Market Agents Blueprint

Go-to-market (GTM) agents help revenue teams prospect, qualify, and accelerate deals while maintaining brand, compliance, and safety.

This blueprint covers:

- Defining GTM agent capabilities, constraints, and success metrics
- Safe data access patterns and policy-as-code permissions
- Evaluation harnesses for tone, relevance, and safety
- Orchestration patterns for outreach, sequencing, and meeting booking
- Closed-loop learning with human feedback and revenue signals

## Architecture
Use layered controls: sandboxed tools, allow-list prompts, and eval-gated releases. Capture telemetry for safety, relevance, and conversion outcomes.

## Implementation steps
1. Scope plays and outcomes (e.g., meeting booked, opportunity advanced)
2. Build eval suites for tone, accuracy, and policy adherence
3. Integrate CRM, calendar, and communication APIs via scoped tokens
4. Roll out with canaries and automated rollback on regressions

## Metrics that matter
- Meeting conversion rate, cycle time reduction, pipeline contribution
- Safety regressions (should trend to zero) and compliance pass rates
