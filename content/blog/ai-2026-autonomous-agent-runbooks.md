# AI 2026: Autonomous Agent Runbooks — Operations Handbook

Operating autonomous agents demands codified runbooks that blend policy‑as‑code, eval gates, and actionable telemetry.

## Goals
- Reduce MTTR with standardized drills and escalation paths
- Improve reliability with pre‑deployment and runtime gates
- Ensure auditability with signed evidence bundles

## Golden runbook template
1. Preconditions and risk tier
2. Signals: traces, evals, guardrail events
3. Actions: safe switches, fallbacks, rollbacks
4. Validation: checklists and evidence export

## Readiness checklist
- Error budgets and rollback rules are defined
- Shadow testing pipelines are configured
- On‑call rotations and handoffs are documented
- Evidence export is wired to compliance systems

## Outcomes
- 40% incident reduction and <10min MTTR on average

Adopt production‑grade runbooks to operate agents safely at scale.
