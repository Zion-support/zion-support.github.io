# AI 2026: Operational Risk Metrics for Agents

Operate autonomous systems with objective, comparable signals:

- Override rate and escalation ratio
- Policy violation density and severity mix
- Groundedness, answerability, and source coverage
- Incident MTTR, rollback latency, and containment time

Adopt a shared scorecard across teams to align goals, budgets, and release decisions.
