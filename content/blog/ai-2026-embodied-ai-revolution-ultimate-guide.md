## What is Embodied AI? The Physical Intelligence Revolution

Embodied AI refers to artificial intelligence systems that exist in and interact with the physical world through robotic bodies, sensors, and actuators. Unlike traditional AI that processes data in digital environments, embodied AI combines:

- **Physical Manipulation**: Robots that can handle, assemble, and manufacture
- **Environmental Awareness**: Sensors that understand spatial relationships
- **Adaptive Learning**: Systems that improve through physical interaction
- **Multi-Modal Intelligence**: Combining vision, touch, sound, and movement

### Key Components of Embodied AI Systems

1. **Robotic Bodies**: Physical hardware capable of manipulation and movement
2. **Sensor Fusion**: Advanced perception combining multiple data streams
3. **AI Brain**: Neural networks trained on physical world interactions
4. **Actuator Control**: Precise manipulation and movement capabilities
5. **Safety Systems**: Fail-safes and human-robot collaboration protocols
