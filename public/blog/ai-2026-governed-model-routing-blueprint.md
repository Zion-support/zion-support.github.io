# AI 2026: Governed Model Routing — Blueprint

Dynamic routing improves outcomes when paired with clear guardrails.

- Route by policy tier, latency budget, and confidence thresholds
- Use shadow traffic and drift monitors to validate changes
- Fall back to safer models or cached results on anomalies
- Record decisions and evidence for audits and continuous improvement

This blueprint aligns spend with reliability without slowing teams down.
