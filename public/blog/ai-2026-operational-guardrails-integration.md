# AI 2026: Operational Guardrails Integration

Reliable autonomy demands operational guardrails that are embedded in day‑to‑day workflows.

- Preflight checks validate models, tools, and data access before changes ship
- Canary and blue/green patterns collect evidence before global rollout
- Live evaluation gates protect user experience and budget in real time
- Incident hooks trigger rollback and posture downgrades automatically

Use these patterns to keep delivery fast while maintaining safety and auditability.
