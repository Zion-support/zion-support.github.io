This executive brief distills the essentials for safe, fast AI agent rollouts:

- Governed tool use with risk tiers and JIT approvals
- Evals wired to gates and incidents
- SLO scorecards guiding deploy, rollback, and routing decisions

What good looks like

- Clear change windows with rollback criteria
- Evidence bundles per release for auditability
- Cost-aware routing to stay within budget without sacrificing quality

Outcomes

- Fewer incidents and faster MTTR
- Predictable spend with policy-aware routing
- Trustworthy autonomy aligned to business objectives
