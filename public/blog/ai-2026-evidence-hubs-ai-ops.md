# AI 2026: Evidence Hubs for AI Ops

Evidence hubs consolidate traces, evaluation results, policy decisions, and incidents into cohesive, portable bundles that support audits, postmortems, and continuous improvement.

## Why now

- Regulatory expectations are increasing
- Autonomy is expanding across critical workflows
- Teams need fast, shared understanding across incidents and releases

## What to include

- Request/response traces with redaction policies
- Evaluation results mapped to SLOs and user outcomes
- Policy decisions and approvals
- Incident links, rollback events, and promotions

## Outcomes

- Faster root cause analysis and safer releases
- Measurable governance without slowing teams
- Durable knowledge for cross‑functional stakeholders
