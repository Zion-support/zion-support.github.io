# Governed Agent Deployments — Ship Fast, Stay Safe

Governed deployments balance speed with accountability. Bake approvals, policy checks, and evidence capture into every step so changes are fast, reversible, and provably compliant.

## Controls that matter

- Change proposals with scoped capability deltas
- Tiered approvals depending on risk and blast radius
- Runtime policy checks and telemetry hooks
- Evidence bundles: traces, evals, policy outcomes, incidents

## Minimal viable governance

Start with a single approval stage, one preflight eval, a canary gate, and rollback criteria. Expand based on incident learnings and SLOs.

## Outcomes

Teams move faster with fewer incidents, and stakeholders get verifiable assurance.
