Enterprises are scaling autonomous agents into production. Without strong posture management, risk compounds quickly. This guide details a pragmatic v2 approach: continuous posture checks, scoped credentials, human-in-the-loop approvals for sensitive actions, and scorecard-driven enforcement tied to SLOs.

Key practices:

1. Risk-tiered capabilities mapped to least-privilege tool adapters.
2. Runtime approvals with auditable evidence bundles for high-impact actions.
3. Live evaluation gates coupled to rollback and incident hooks.
4. Drift detection with posture scorecards and automated remediation.

Implementation blueprint covers CI/CD preflight, runtime decision points, and incident-aware rollbacks.
