# Production Evidence Hubs — Blueprint (2026)

Operational reliability depends on verifiable evidence:

- Minimal trace schema with privacy-aware fields
- Artifact bundling for decisions and outcomes
- Policy event stream with approvals and exceptions
- Scorecards that align to business metrics

This blueprint shows how to wire evidence into delivery pipelines.
