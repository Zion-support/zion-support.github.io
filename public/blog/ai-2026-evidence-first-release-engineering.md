# AI 2026: Evidence‑First Release Engineering

Operationalize reliability by treating evidence as a first‑class artifact alongside code.

## Core Practices
- Preflight checks produce signed scorecards
- Canary rollouts emit structured traces tied to change IDs
- Runtime guardrails log policy decisions and tool envelopes

## Evidence Bundle
- Traces + evaluations + policy events + incidents
- Linked to release, owner, and rollback contract

## Outcomes
- Faster incident triage and lower MTTR
- Clear compliance trail without slowing teams
