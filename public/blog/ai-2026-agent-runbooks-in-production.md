Agent operations benefit from runbooks enriched with live evidence:

Runbook anatomy

1. Trigger conditions mapped to SLOs and eval thresholds
2. Policy-aware rollback and change freeze steps
3. Evidence capture: traces, evals, policy events, and incident notes

Implementation tips

- Use scorecards for on-call decision support
- Automate workaround playbooks while long-term fixes are tracked
- Capture outcomes to continuously improve evals and policies
