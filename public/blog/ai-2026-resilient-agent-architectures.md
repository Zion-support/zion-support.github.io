# Resilient Agent Architectures (2026)

Reliability emerges from architecture: fallbacks, timeouts, circuit breakers, and bounded tool scopes.

## Core patterns

- Guardrailed tool adapters with risk tiers
- Scorecard-driven canary and rollout
- Hybrid retrieval with backpressure
- Adaptive model routing and budgets

## Ops hooks

Wire incidents, evals, and policy events at the seams. Treat evidence as a first-class product.
