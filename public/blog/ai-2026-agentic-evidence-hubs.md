# AI 2026: Agentic Evidence Hubs

Evidence hubs unify the signals needed to prove safety, quality, and cost control.

- Streamlined traces capture key spans with minimal overhead
- Evaluation results are attached to deployments, incidents, and rollbacks
- Policy events and approvals are stored with context for audits
- Scorecards summarize the posture and outcomes across releases

Adopt evidence hubs to make reliability measurable and governance actionable.
