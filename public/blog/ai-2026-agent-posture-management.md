# AI 2026: Real-Time Agent Posture Management

Operational posture combines capability, permission, and context to determine what an agent can safely do right now.

- Scorecards track risk, confidence, and recent violations
- Policies gate access to tools and data
- Posture shifts dynamically based on telemetry and incidents
- Fallback modes degrade gracefully to protect outcomes and budget

Use this guide to wire posture checks into orchestration and delivery.
