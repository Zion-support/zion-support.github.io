# Governed Real-Time Tooling Approvals (2026)

Sensitive actions require approvals, but approvals should not stall delivery. This post shows a pragmatic path.

## Approvals that scale

- Risk-tiered actions with scoped credentials
- Auto-approve low-risk; require quorum for high-risk
- Persist policy events and attach evidence

## Implementation

Use signed intents with time-bound tokens. Log outcomes and tie to post-incident reviews.
