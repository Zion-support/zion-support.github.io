# AI 2026: Evidence Bundles — Executive Guide

Evidence bundles package the who/what/why of AI behavior for every release, decision, and incident.

- Link model inputs/outputs to evaluations and policy events
- Capture artifacts and counterfactuals for reviews
- Enable fact-based rollbacks and improvements

Leaders get clarity; teams get repeatable, auditable workflows.
