# Operational Evaluation Maturity Model

This guide outlines a pragmatic path for teams to evolve from ad-hoc checks to production-grade, evidence-first evaluation gates. It includes milestones, example SLIs/SLOs, and rollout patterns that minimize delivery friction while improving reliability.

## What you will learn

- How to define outcomes and evidence for agentic systems
- How to stage eval gates across preflight, canary, and post-release
- How to measure impact without slowing teams down

## Quickstart

1. Instrument traces and artifacts for verifiability
2. Add preflight checks for groundedness and safety
3. Introduce canary with rollback conditions and alerts
4. Track post-release SLIs tied to business KPIs

Use the checklists to pilot in one squad this week.
