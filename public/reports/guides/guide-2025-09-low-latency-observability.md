# Guide — Low‑Latency Observability for Agentic Systems

This guide shows how to capture the minimum viable evidence for reliability while keeping costs and latency under control.

- Define outcome‑aligned SLIs and wire to SLOs
- Use lean traces with privacy filters and late enrichment
- Stream metrics for instant signal without heavy sampling costs

Adopt a staged rollout: instrument, validate signals, enforce SLOs, then automate rollback.
