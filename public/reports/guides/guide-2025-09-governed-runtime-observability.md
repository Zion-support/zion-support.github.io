# Governed Runtime Observability (2026)

Low-latency telemetry and evidence-first traces keep autonomous systems reliable and auditable.

- Instrument agent actions with tool, risk tier, and approval context
- Add evaluation hooks for groundedness, policy violations, and utility
- Capture policy events, incidents, and rollback decisions per release

Bundle results into an evidence package that informs scorecards, approvals, and postmortems.
