# Executive Summary — Evaluation Gates

Evaluation gates provide a lightweight, evidence-first mechanism to keep quality high without slowing teams. This brief covers the minimum viable guardrails to pilot in 30 days.

## TL;DR

- Align SLIs to business outcomes; avoid vanity metrics
- Start with preflight checks; add canary and rollback
- Monitor cost, latency, and quality holistically
