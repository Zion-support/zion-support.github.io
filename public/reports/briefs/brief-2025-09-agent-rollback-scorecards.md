# Brief — Agent Rollback with Evaluation Scorecards

Rollback should be a product decision informed by evidence, not an ad‑hoc panic.

- Define scorecards with thresholds linked to business KPIs
- Trigger staged rollback when safety or quality degrades
- Capture artifacts for postmortems and learning loops

Use approvals and audit trails to make rollbacks safe and repeatable.
