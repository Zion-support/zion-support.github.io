# 41% Incident Reduction with Evaluation Gates

By introducing preflight groundedness checks and a canary stage with rollback, a platform team reduced customer-facing incidents by 41% month-over-month while maintaining delivery speed. This case study details the rollout, metrics, and lessons learned.

## Key results

- 41% fewer incidents; 32% faster MTTR
- No statistically significant impact on lead time
- Increased confidence in shipping agentic features

## Approach

Start small with one service, define rollback conditions, and expand.
