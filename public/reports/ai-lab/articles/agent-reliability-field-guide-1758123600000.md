# Agent Reliability Field Guide (2026)

A practical, battle-tested field guide for keeping agentic systems reliable in production.

## Core Practices
- Evidence-first observability: traces, artifacts, policy events
- Online evaluation gates tied to SLOs
- Budget-aware routing and graceful degradation

## Incident Readiness
- Scorecard-driven runbooks and approvals
- Containment playbooks and kill-switches
- Postmortems with reproducible evidence

## Continuous Improvement
- Close the loop from incidents to tests
- Expand scenario coverage from real-world data
- Align SLI changes to product outcomes
