🔌 Troubleshooting Tips

  1. Check if your device is connected to Wi-Fi or mobile data
  2. Try turning your Wi-Fi off and on again
  3. Move closer to your router if using Wi-Fi
  4. Check if other websites are working
  5. Restart your device if the problem persists
