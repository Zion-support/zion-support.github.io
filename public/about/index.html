Our Story

Founded in 2020, Zion Tech Group emerged from a vision to bridge the gap between cutting-edge research and practical business applications. Our founders recognized that the most revolutionary technologies were often inaccessible to the businesses that needed them most.

Starting with a small team of AI researchers and quantum physicists, we began developing autonomous systems that could transform how businesses operate. Today, we're proud to serve over 50 enterprise clients across industries, from healthcare to finance, manufacturing to space exploration.

Our journey has been marked by breakthrough innovations in AI autonomous systems, quantum computing applications, and space technology. We've consistently pushed the boundaries of what's possible, always with a focus on practical, scalable solutions that drive real business value.
