## A pragmatic risk lens

Not all agentic features are equal. A simple read‑only assistant carries different risk than an autonomous tool‑using agent. This matrix helps teams pick controls proportional to risk.

## Dimensions

- Capability: read‑only, tool‑using, multi‑step autonomous
- Scope: data sensitivity, blast radius, external actions
- Impact: user harm potential, financial loss, regulatory exposure

## Controls by tier

- Low: logging, basic evals, denial‑by‑default
- Medium: approvals, budget limits, recovery playbooks
- High: dual control, realtime policy checks, shadow mode first

## Implementation notes

- Start conservative and relax with evidence
- Tie controls to SLOs and incidents, not opinions
- Keep the matrix living—revisit with each major release
