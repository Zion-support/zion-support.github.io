## Why scorecards for agent observability

Traditional dashboards are noisy. Executives and incident commanders need a single, decision‑ready view that translates traces, evals, and policy outcomes into clear signals.

### Core signals

- Groundedness: hallucination and faithfulness evals over time
- Safety: policy violations by capability and severity
- Reliability: SLO compliance, latency percentiles, failure modes
- Cost: token burn, cache hit rate, routing efficiency

### Implementation blueprint

1. Normalize events from traces, eval runners, and policy engines into a unified schema.
2. Aggregate to service, capability, and release levels with time windows.
3. Define red/amber/green thresholds per metric with owners and runbooks.
4. Wire alerts to on‑call and release gates; link incidents and postmortems.

### Outcome

Leaders get a weekly rollup and real‑time release gates that keep autonomy fast, safe, and on budget.
