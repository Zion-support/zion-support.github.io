## Operate autonomy with evidence at the center

Shift from hope‑based to evidence‑led operations. Every autonomous action should be justified by traces, gated by evals, and bounded by runtime guardrails.

### Core practices

- Eval gates: block releases and risky actions when quality drops
- Structured traces: correlate tool calls, approvals, and policy outcomes
- Incident links: connect violations to postmortems and learning loops
- Budgets: token and time budgets with alerts and auto‑throttling

### Daily workflow

1. Review scorecards and incident digests each morning.
2. Triage regressions with replayable traces and reproducible evals.
3. Land fixes behind gates; canary and auto‑rollback on violations.
4. Capture evidence bundles per release for audit readiness.

Evidence‑led ops keeps teams confident to ship autonomy faster without sacrificing safety.
