Multi‑agent workflows amplify both impact and risk. Standardize tool risk tiers, scoped credentials, per‑tool budgets, and JIT approvals. Capture evidence for every critical action.

Operational checklist:
1. Per‑tool risk tiers and policy envelopes
2. Scoped tokens and short‑lived creds
3. Budget and latency guards per agent chain
4. Runtime eval gates and incident linking
5. Postmortems with evidence bundles
