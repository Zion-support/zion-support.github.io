## Modernization pillars

- Event‑driven backbone with clear ownership and contracts
- Evidence‑led quality gates and operational SLOs
- Policy‑aware services that emit auditable traces

### Migration playbook

Start with high‑change domains. Introduce event streams, carve out anti‑corruption layers, and add eval/SLO scorecards to every release. Tie budget to reliability outcomes.
