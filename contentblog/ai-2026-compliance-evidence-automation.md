Manual audit prep does not scale. Automate evidence collection across the AI lifecycle to reduce friction and cost.

Core mechanisms:

1. Policy‑as‑code — Codify controls and link them to runtime checks and evals.
2. Evidence hooks — Emit traces, policy events, and incidents with release IDs.
3. Scorecards — Roll evidence into objective pass/fail with thresholds per control.
4. Approvals — Gate risky changes with JIT reviewer workflows and budgets.
5. Storage — Immutable, queryable evidence for audits and customer transparency.

Benefit: Faster reviews, fewer surprises, and trustworthy releases.
