Cost awareness is a product feature. Bake it into the agent lifecycle.

## Tactics

- Route by difficulty and confidence bands
- Cache embeddings and tool outputs
- Batch requests and stream responses
- Evaluate model/parameter tradeoffs regularly

Track spend per scenario and enforce budgets with policy.
