Reliability for autonomous systems depends on trustworthy evidence. Integrate:

- Evals at PR, canary, and runtime gates
- Policy‑as‑code decisions and rule hits
- Incident timelines with agent context and tool approvals

Wire to scorecards with SLOs for safety and override rate. Use budgets and approvals on high‑risk tools and centralize artifacts for audits.
