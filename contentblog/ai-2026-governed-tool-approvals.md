Governed tool use should be fast and safe. The key is risk‑tiering with automation first, approvals only when needed.

Blueprint:

1. Classify tools and actions by risk (read, write, external network, financial)
2. Define budgets and quotas with automatic cutoffs and alerts
3. Apply policy‑as‑code with simulation mode before enforcement
4. Require human approval for high‑risk actions with full context attached
5. Log everything to the Evidence Hub for audits and retrospectives

This approach minimizes friction while meeting safety, compliance, and cost objectives.
