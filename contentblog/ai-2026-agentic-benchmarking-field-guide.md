Benchmarking agentic systems requires more than classic ML metrics.

You need:

1. Scenario suites that mirror production workflows
2. SLIs and SLOs aligned to business outcomes
3. Safety, reliability, and cost signals captured as first-class evidence

This guide walks through:

- Defining task graphs and critical paths
- Measuring override rates, violation rates, and groundedness
- Capturing traces, evals, and policy events for audit-ready artifacts
- Running scorecards for release gates and ongoing health

By operationalizing benchmarks as code, teams can ship faster with confidence.
