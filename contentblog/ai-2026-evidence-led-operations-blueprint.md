Operating AI systems at scale demands decisions anchored in evidence. This blueprint shows how to centralize live evals, incidents, approvals, and business KPIs into a single, queryable hub that informs change management, rollbacks, and postmortems.

## Why evidence‑led operations

- Drive releases with objective eval gates instead of intuition
- Shorten incident MTTR with unified traces and safety telemetry
- Improve trust with auditable approvals and policy‑as‑code

## Core components

1. Evaluation harness with scenario suites and policy gates
2. Safety and performance telemetry stitched to each request
3. Incident ingestion with standardized taxonomy and severities
4. Approval workflows tied to risk tier and evidence bundles
5. Executive KPIs (ROI, cost/task, reliability) aligned to SLOs

## Rollout playbook

1. Baseline: define top journeys and SLOs
2. Instrument: add traces, evals, and safety checks
3. Gate: enforce pass thresholds in CI/CD and progressive delivery
4. Observe: stream metrics to dashboards and alerts
5. Improve: run weekly evidence reviews and ship guarded changes

## Outcomes

- Gate pass rate > 95%
- Rollback time < 5 minutes
- Change failure rate < 3%
- Audit‑ready artifacts for each release

Adopt evidence‑led operations to move fast, stay safe, and continuously improve with confidence.
