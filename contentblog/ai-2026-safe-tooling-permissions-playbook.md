Principles:

1. Risk tiers with default‑deny for high‑impact actions
2. Just‑in‑time credentials and time‑boxed scopes
3. Budget and rate limits with anomaly triggers
4. Evidence capture: who/what/why for every action

Outcome: controlled autonomy with audit‑ready trails.
