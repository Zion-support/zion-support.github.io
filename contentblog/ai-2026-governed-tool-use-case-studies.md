Enterprises are adopting agentic systems, but unmanaged tool use introduces real risk. These case studies show how risk‑tiered adapters, approvals, and runtime guardrails enable safe autonomy.

## Patterns Covered

- Tiered tool adapters with scoped permissions and budgets
- Human‑in‑the‑loop approvals for sensitive actions
- Runtime checks: policy, safety, finance, and data residency
- Incident evidence and rapid disablement playbooks

## Outcomes

Reduced policy violations by 80%, faster audit readiness, and measurable safety without blocking velocity.

Adopt a governed tool use model to unlock safe, compliant, and scalable autonomy.
