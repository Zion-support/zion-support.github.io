## Why zero-trust for agents

Human zero‑trust patterns translate well to autonomous systems: assume breach, minimize blast radius, continuously verify, and log everything.

### Core practices

- Risk‑tiered tooling with explicit allowlists and safe adapters
- Scoped, short‑lived credentials and environment segmentation
- Just‑in‑time human approvals for sensitive actions
- Policy evaluation on every tool invocation with audit trails

### Rollout blueprint

1. Classify tools and actions by risk.
2. Wrap tools with policy enforcement and structured logging.
3. Issue ephemeral credentials via a broker.
4. Add approval workflows and alerting for high‑risk paths.

These controls make autonomy ship‑ready without stalling velocity.
