Use this checklist to stand up governed agent operations with clear controls and evidence.

## Foundations

- Risk tiers define tool scopes, budgets, and approval paths
- SLIs/SLOs set for safety, reliability, quality, latency, and cost
- Incident taxonomy includes policy and eval regressions

## Runtime Controls

- Policy‑as‑code for prompts, tools, data access
- Real‑time eval gates with deterministic fallbacks
- Budget and timeout guards per request and session

## Evidence & Audit

- Traces capture inputs, outputs, tool calls, and cost
- Evals, policy events, and incidents stored per release
- Executive scorecards summarize trust signals and ROI

Ship autonomy faster—with proof it’s safe and within budget.
