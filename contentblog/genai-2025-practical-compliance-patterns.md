Shipping GenAI in regulated environments requires a blend of robust evaluations, guardrails, and operational controls. This guide covers:

1. Evaluations that matter: factuality, safety, bias, PII leakage.
2. Data governance: immutable audit logs, role-based redaction, and traceability.
3. Human-in-the-loop approvals for high-risk actions.
4. Real-time monitoring and policy-driven rollbacks.

Adopt a layered defense: preventive (prompt filters), detective (evals + telemetry), and corrective (kill switches). Start small, measure, and iterate.
