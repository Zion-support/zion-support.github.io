Agentic workflows in CRM automate repetitive steps while preserving human control. Core components:

- Intent detection and routing
- Playbook selection based on buyer stage
- Safety checks and approvals for outbound actions
- Continuous evaluations to reduce hallucinations

Start with low-risk automations (enrichment, research) and graduate to outreach/renewals with clear stop conditions.
