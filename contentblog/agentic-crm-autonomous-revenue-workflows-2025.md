## Why agentic RevOps now

Revenue operations increasingly rely on multi-app workflows. Agentic patterns let you orchestrate CRM updates, enrichment, outreach, and follow-ups with policy-safe autonomy.

## Blueprint

1. Triggers: lead created, stage changed, intent signal detected.
2. Retrieve context: CRM, product analytics, billing, conversations.
3. Decide: evaluate playbook with guardrails and SLAs.
4. Act: update CRM, schedule tasks, send messages, open tickets.
5. Verify: evals and feedback loops ensure quality and compliance.

## Implementation notes

- Use evaluations to continuously measure win-rate lift and response latency.
- Add human-in-the-loop for sensitive steps like pricing or discounts.
- Log actions with structured events for auditability.

## Next steps

- Start with a narrow segment and scale.
- Ship a dry-run mode before enabling writes.
- Pair with an LLM risk policy and red-team tests.
