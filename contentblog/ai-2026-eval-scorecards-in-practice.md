Evaluation scorecards translate noisy metrics into crisp decisions. A practical scorecard balances four lenses: Reliability, Safety, Quality, and Cost.

Implementation steps:

- Define business‑aligned SLIs and thresholds per route or capability
- Aggregate scenario‑level results into tiered scorecards (team ➝ product ➝ portfolio)
- Weight high‑risk scenarios and customer‑visible flows more heavily
- Wire scorecards to promotion gates, rollback policies, and on‑call dashboards

Visualization tips:

- Use stoplight visuals, trend lines, and percentile bands
- Provide drill‑downs to scenarios, traces, and raw evidence
- Export static snapshots per release for audits

Teams that use scorecards consistently ship faster with fewer incidents and clearer tradeoffs.
