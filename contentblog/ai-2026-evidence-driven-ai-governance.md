## Operational governance in practice

Policies are only useful when they drive real decisions. Evidence‑driven governance centralizes traces, evals, policy events, and incidents into a single evidence hub that supports approvals, reviews, and audits.

## What to collect

- Policy decisions and rationales
- Safety and quality evaluation results
- Model/router versions and change logs
- Incident reports and corrective actions

## Core capabilities

- Evidence schemas with lineage and immutability
- Approval workflows tied to risk tiers and SLAs
- Dashboards for reliability, safety, and cost envelopes

## Quick start

1. Define an evidence schema and retention policy
2. Integrate pipelines to capture evals and policy events
3. Roll out approval flows for high‑risk changes

## Benefits

- Faster, safer releases with clear accountability
- Lower audit burden with ready‑made evidence
- Alignment between builders, risk, and leadership
