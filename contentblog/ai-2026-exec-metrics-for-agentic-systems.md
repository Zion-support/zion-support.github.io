Executives need a concise view of agent performance, risk, and ROI. This guide maps telemetry to board‑level metrics.

## Core Metrics

- Reliability: task success rate, override rate, timeouts
- Safety: policy violation rate, containment effectiveness, escalation MTTA/MTTR
- Quality: groundedness, hallucination suppression, review rejection rate
- Efficiency: cost per task, latency percentiles, budget adherence

## Scorecards

Adopt a balanced scorecard with SLO targets, trends, and incident links. Pair leading indicators (guardrail triggers) with lagging ones (customer impact).

## Operating Rhythm

Weekly reviews on regressions and risk items, monthly budget alignment, quarterly roadmap based on eval gaps and incident learnings.

This standardization accelerates safe autonomy while keeping leadership in control.
