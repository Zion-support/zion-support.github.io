Dynamic routing selects the right model path per request:

- Inputs: budget, latency target, quality threshold, risk tier
- Signals: cached answers, retrieval hits, eval probes, live SLOs
- Actions: pick model, enable speculative decode, degrade safely

Start with a conservative baseline and add policies that cap spend while preserving minimum quality. Track overrides and violations with scorecards.
