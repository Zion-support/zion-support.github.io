Scorecards align leadership with engineering by converting abstract safety goals into measurable metrics. This article covers:

- Policy families and severity mapping
- Weighted compliance scoring per task class
- Real-time dashboards and alerts
- Release gates keyed to minimum safety scores

Example metrics:

- Tool permission violations per 1k actions
- Sensitive data exposure incidents per week
- Human confirmation rate for high-risk actions

Adopt scorecards to make safety visible, actionable, and continuously improving.
