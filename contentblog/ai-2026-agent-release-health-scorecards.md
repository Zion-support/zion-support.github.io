Release health for agentic systems must blend product analytics with AI‑specific signals. Traditional web and mobile scorecards miss jailbreak rates, groundedness, tool‑safety violations, and inference budget adherence.

Key metrics:
- Jailbreak/override rate
- Groundedness score
- Tool‑use violation rate by risk tier
- Cost per task and budget adherence
- Regression rate gated by evals

Adopt a single executive view that rolls up per‑release signals and links to incidents, postmortems, and policy changes.
