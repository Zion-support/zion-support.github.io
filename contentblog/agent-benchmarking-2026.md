Shipping agent systems to production demands clear, repeatable evaluation. In this guide, we cover:

- Why offline evals are not enough for agent workflows
- The difference between capability, robustness, and safety checks
- How to define task-specific success metrics and guardrails
- A reference pipeline for eval-gated shipping

We also include a starter checklist and a minimal rubric template that you can adapt to your stack.
