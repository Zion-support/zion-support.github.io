Modern AI systems evolve continuously. This blueprint outlines how to run safe, auditable change across prompts, tools, policies, and routing without slowing delivery.

Key practices:

1. Evidence‑gated releases — Require green evals, policy checks, and budget adherence for promotion.
2. Risk‑tiered rollouts — Start with shadow/testing, then canaries with automatic aborts on violations.
3. Runbook automation — Encode rollback and escalation paths; exercise them via game days.
4. Change windows and ownership — Track accountable owners, approvers, and incident links.
5. Immutable evidence — Store traces, evals, incidents, and approvals with release IDs.

Outcome: Faster iteration with lower incident rates and audit‑ready proof of control.
