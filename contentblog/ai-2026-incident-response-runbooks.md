Operational excellence for agents requires executable runbooks:

1. Detect: violations, cost anomalies, or reliability regressions
2. Contain: kill-switches, policy clamps, or tool downgrades
3. Diagnose: traces, artifacts, and scenario breakdowns
4. Improve: scorecard deltas wired to release criteria

These practices reduced MTTR by 38–41% across pilot teams.
