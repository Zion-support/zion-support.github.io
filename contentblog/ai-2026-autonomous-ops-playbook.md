Build self-healing systems by combining:

1. SLOs and error budgets as guardrails for agent actions
2. Policy checks for infra/app changes
3. Offline and online evaluations for decision quality
4. Progressive delivery with auto-rollback

Pilot in staging and measure MTTD/MTTR improvements before expanding to production.
