## Why customer agents now

Customer-facing AI agents reduce wait times, personalize support, and unlock 24/7 coverage without linear headcount. With improved tool-use and evaluations, teams can launch safely and iterate quickly.

### Value levers

- Deflect repetitive tickets with high-resolution answers
- Accelerate revenue with guided selling and next-best-action
- Capture rich product feedback directly from conversations

## Architecture blueprint

1. Retrieval layer with domain-verified sources
2. Policy/guardrails for safety and brand tone
3. Tooling for actions (CRM, order status, billing)
4. Observability: traces, metrics, and chat replays
5. Continuous evaluations: correctness, safety, CX

## Safety and governance

Adopt policy-as-code for what agents can say and do. Use red-teaming and regression suites to prevent drift. Route risky requests to humans with clear handoff.

## Measuring ROI

Track first-contact resolution, CSAT, containment rate, and assisted revenue. Tie outcomes to cohorts and product areas to prioritize improvements.

## Launch checklist

- Golden datasets and eval baselines in CI
- Fail-safe fallbacks and escalation
- Domain monitors for hallucinations
- Playbooks for updates and rollbacks
