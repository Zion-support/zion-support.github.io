Risk‑tiered tooling lets you scale agent capabilities without sacrificing safety.

Core practices:

- Classify tools by risk tier (read, write, financial, destructive)
- Require approvals and budgets for sensitive tiers
- Enforce runtime guardrails and capture policy events

Pair this with eval gates and incident response to create a safe envelope for autonomy.
