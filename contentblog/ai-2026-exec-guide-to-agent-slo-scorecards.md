## What leaders need

Executives need a single lens on reliability, safety, and value. SLO scorecards turn technical signals into business‑aligned commitments and red/green health.

### Scorecard ingredients

- Reliability: successful task completion, rollback rate, MTTR
- Safety: override rate, violation rate, tool misuse incidents
- Quality: groundedness, factuality, user satisfaction proxies
- Efficiency: latency budgets, cost per task, cache hit rate

### Operating with SLOs

- Gate releases on score thresholds and trending
- Tie incidents to SLO breaches with clear ownership
- Publish weekly deltas and corrective actions

Outcome: faster iteration with shared truth between engineers and executives.
